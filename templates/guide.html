‚≠ê Reputation System

Your actions affect how NPCs perceive you. Being polite, giving gifts, and helping NPCs improves your reputation. Negative actions lower it.

  • High Reputation: Better prices, friendlier NPCs, special dialogue
  • Low Reputation: Higher prices, suspicious NPCs, limited interactions
  • NPCs Remember: AI-enhanced NPCs remember your past conversations and actions
üí° Tip: Use polite language, give items to NPCs, and engage in friendly conversation to build reputation. Check your reputation by using 'look me' to see your character stats.

üèõÔ∏è The World of Hollowvale

Hollowvale is a frontier town with several key locations:

  • Hollowvale Town Square: The heart of the town, where many paths converge
  • The Rusty Tankard Tavern: A warm gathering place run by Mara, the innkeeper
  • Old Stoneforge Smithy: Where metalwork is crafted
  • Market Lane: A quiet lane where vendors set up their wares
  • Shrine of the Forgotten Path: An ancient place of mystery and power
  • The Whispering Wood: A dense forest that seems alive
  • Old Watchtower: A vantage point overlooking the valley
üí° Tip: Explore everywhere! Each location has its own character, items, and NPCs. Some places hold secrets waiting to be discovered.

ü§ñ AI-Enhanced NPCs

Some NPCs in Hollowvale are powered by AI, making conversations dynamic and memorable:

  • They remember your past conversations
  • They react to your reputation
  • They respond contextually to what you say
  • They can understand natural language purchases
  • They move between rooms and react to weather
üí° Tip: Try having real conversations with AI NPCs! Ask them questions, tell them about yourself, or just chat. They'll respond in character based on their personality.
