The room is a dim sanctuary where shadows pool like ink in the crevices of ancient stone. Shelves carved from the very bones of the earth cradle artifacts, each whispering secrets in the soft hiss of rustling parchment. The only light filters through a stained glass window, casting trembling patterns on the floor, like the ethereal dance of forgotten memories.
