### Room List with Exits and Design Notes

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits**: North to [General], Northeast to [Curio Shop]
   - **Purpose**: Central hub of the Merchant District; bustling with vendors, perfect for player interaction and trade opportunities.
   - **Design Notes**: The open space encourages social activities and serves as a primary gathering point. It naturally leads players to explore adjacent shops.

2. **General Store**
   - **Exits**: South to [Market Square], down to [Warehouse]
   - **Purpose**: Sells everyday goods; a logical first stop for adventurers.
   - **Design Notes**: Positioned to draw players from the Market Square, offering essential supplies. The connection to the Warehouse provides a seamless flow of goods narrative.

3. **Curio Shop**
   - **Exits**: Southwest to [Market Square], South to [Alleyway]
   - **Purpose**: Offers rare and mysterious items, appealing to treasure hunters and lore enthusiasts.
   - **Design Notes**: The slightly out-of-the-way location adds an air of secrecy and allure, while the connection to an alley suggests hidden dealings.

4. **Blacksmith**
   - **Exits**: North to [General], East to [Alleyway]
   - **Purpose**: Forge and repair weapons and armor; essential for player preparation.
   - **Design Notes**: Situated near the General Store for logical supply flow, with an exit to the Alleyway for potential side quests or illicit activities.

5. **Warehouse**
   - **Exits**: Up to [General]
   - **Purpose**: Storage for goods; potential for quests involving missing shipments or hidden contraband.
   - **Design Notes**: This room uses vertical space to create a sense of depth and realism in how goods are managed within the district.

6. **Alleyway**
   - **Exits**: North to [Curio Shop], West to [Blacksmith], East to [Hidden Path]
   - **Purpose**: A shadowy thoroughfare for clandestine meetings and secretive transactions.
   - **Design Notes**: Provides a shortcut and introduces a sense of danger and intrigue. The Hidden Path exit rewards exploration and leads to an undisclosed area, suggesting further mysteries.

### Overall Design Notes

- **Flow**: The Market Square acts as the central hub, facilitating easy access to essential services while encouraging exploration of less obvious paths.
- **Discovery**: The Hidden Path and Alleyway offer layers of complexity and reward players who seek out the less-traveled routes.
- **Pacing**: The district design clusters commerce-related rooms, with more secretive or optional areas branching off, balancing convenience with the thrill of discovery.
- **Realism**: The layout mimics a logical city district, with stores central to trade and back alleys hinting at the underbelly of urban life.
