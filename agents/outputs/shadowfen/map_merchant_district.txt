### Merchant District in Shadowfen Style

```
[Market Square]--[General Store]--[Tailor Shop]
     |                |
[Alchemist Shop]    [Hidden Alley]
     |
[Blacksmith Forge]

```

#### Room List with Exits

1. **Market Square**
   - Exits: North to General Store, East to Alchemist Shop
   - Notes: Central hub of the Merchant District, bustling with traders and shoppers. A lively atmosphere with stalls selling various goods. Serves as the main gathering point and orientation center for players.

2. **General Store**
   - Exits: South to Market Square, East to Tailor Shop, South to Hidden Alley
   - Notes: A one-stop shop for adventurers' basic needs, from provisions to simple equipment. Positioned between the Market Square and Tailor Shop, it naturally channels players exploring retail spaces.

3. **Tailor Shop**
   - Exits: West to General Store
   - Notes: Specializes in clothing and armor customization. Located at the edge of the district, encouraging players to explore the surrounding areas for more specialized services.

4. **Alchemist Shop**
   - Exits: West to Market Square, South to Blacksmith Forge
   - Notes: Sells potions and magical ingredients. Adjacent to the Market Square for easy access by players, with a secondary path leading down to the Blacksmith Forge, promoting vertical exploration.

5. **Hidden Alley**
   - Exits: North to General Store
   - Notes: A secretive, narrow passage accessible only through the General Store, offering hidden quests or black market dealings. Rewards players who explore thoroughly with unique opportunities or items.

6. **Blacksmith Forge**
   - Exits: North to Alchemist Shop
   - Notes: Located one level down from the Alchemist Shop, providing a sense of depth and verticality. Specializes in weapons and armor crafting. The forge's heat and noise contrast sharply with the magical ambiance of the Alchemist Shop above.

#### Design Notes

- **Flow**: The Market Square serves as the district's central hub, naturally directing players to essential services like the General Store and Alchemist Shop. The Tailor Shop, positioned further out, encourages exploration.
  
- **Discovery**: The Hidden Alley provides a secret path and a sense of mystery, rewarding players who investigate thoroughly with unique content.

- **Pacing**: Clustering the General Store and Tailor Shop near the Market Square focuses commerce-related activities while spreading out specialized services like the Blacksmith Forge, providing a balanced exploration experience.

- **Realism**: The layout reflects a logical organization, with essential services like the General Store easily accessible from the Market Square, while specialty shops are slightly more remote, mirroring realistic urban planning.

- **Vertical Space**: The connection between the Alchemist Shop and Blacksmith Forge introduces vertical exploration, adding complexity and depth to the district.
