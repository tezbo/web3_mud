**Room List & Exits:**

1. **Watch Tower (Top)**
   - **Exits**: Down to Watch Tower
   - **Purpose**: Observation point providing a view over the district. Acts as a high vantage point for guards and a strategic location for players seeking an overview.

2. **Watch Tower**
   - **Exits**: Up to Watch Tower (Top), Down to Market Square
   - **Purpose**: Central hub for patrols, providing security and oversight for the district. Encourages players to visit for quests related to district safety.

3. **Market Square**
   - **Exits**: Up to Watch Tower, East to General Store, South to Inn, West to Blacksmith, Southwest to Alchemist, Southeast to Fish Market
   - **Purpose**: The bustling heart of the Merchant District, where various traders and vendors gather. Designed to be a central hub with easy access to all major commercial locations.

4. **Blacksmith**
   - **Exits**: East to Market Square
   - **Purpose**: Provides crafting and repair services. Positioned close to the market for convenience but separated to prevent noise from disturbing other merchants.

5. **General Store**
   - **Exits**: West to Market Square
   - **Purpose**: Supplies adventurers with essential goods. Centrally located to be directly accessible from the Market Square, facilitating easy trade.

6. **Alchemist**
   - **Exits**: Northeast to Market Square
   - **Purpose**: Offers potions and unique items. Located slightly off the main path to create a sense of discovery for players seeking special goods.

7. **Inn**
   - **Exits**: North to Market Square
   - **Purpose**: Provides lodging and a place for social interaction. Positioned near the Market Square to serve weary travelers and those needing rest.

8. **Fish Market**
   - **Exits**: Northwest to Market Square
   - **Purpose**: Sells fresh seafood, highlighting the district's proximity to water. Placed at the edge of the Market Square to manage the distinct smell from affecting other merchants.

**Design Notes:**

- **Flow & Centrality**: The Market Square acts as the primary hub, ensuring that all key locations are easily accessible while maintaining a realistic sense of a bustling district.
  
- **Exploration & Discovery**: The Alchemist and Fish Market are slightly off the beaten path, rewarding players who take the time to explore beyond the obvious routes.

- **Vertical Space**: The Watch Tower provides a vertical element, utilizing the up/down dimensions to add depth and complexity to the district's navigation.

- **Realism & Storytelling**: The layout reflects a logical organization where shops and services are arranged for both convenience and thematic coherence, with the Watch Tower symbolizing authority and oversight characteristic of a Shadowfen style.
