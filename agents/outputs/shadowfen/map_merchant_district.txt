### Room List with Exits

1. **Market Square**
   - Exits: North to Tailor, East to Smith, South to Alchemist
   - Purpose: Central hub for the Merchant District, high-traffic area where players gather. Various stalls and merchants offer a wide array of goods. Provides a sense of bustling activity.

2. **Alchemist's Shop**
   - Exits: North to Market Square, South to Inn
   - Purpose: A quaint shop filled with mysterious potions and ingredients. Offers unique items for players interested in alchemy and magic. Positioned to encourage exploration between essential locations.

3. **Tailor**
   - Exits: South to Market Square
   - Purpose: A specialized shop for clothing and fabric-based equipment. Adds variety to the district's offerings, showcasing the diversity of merchants.

4. **Smith**
   - Exits: West to Market Square
   - Purpose: A place for players to acquire weapons and armor. Close proximity to the market emphasizes its importance to adventurers gearing up for quests.

5. **Inn**
   - Exits: North to Alchemist, South to Dock
   - Purpose: Provides lodging and rest for weary travelers. Positioned as a transition between the bustling district and the quieter dock area. Encourages players to pause and plan their next steps.

6. **Dock**
   - Exits: North to Inn
   - Purpose: A serene waterfront area where goods are shipped in and out. Offers a picturesque contrast to the busy market, a potential starting point for quests involving trade or travel.

### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose

- **Central Hub**: The Market Square serves as the central hub, with key shops like the Smith and Alchemist's Shop nearby, ensuring players can easily access important services.
- **Natural Discovery**: The layout encourages players to naturally discover the Tailor and Dock as they move through the district, rewarding exploration with varied experiences.
- **Logical Organization**: Shops are situated logically around the square, with related services (e.g., Smith and Alchemist) positioned nearby to enhance flow without overwhelming players.
- **Pacing**: The Dock provides a change of pace from the busy district, acting as a peaceful endpoint that invites players to contemplate future adventures.
- **Realism**: The layout makes physical sense, with the Inn acting as a logical transition between the commerce-heavy areas and the more serene Dock, reflecting real-world urban planning.
