**Design Notes:**

- **Flow and Purpose:** The Marketplace serves as the central hub, naturally guiding players towards important shops. The Apothecary and Weapon Smith are positioned to cater to players interested in crafting and combat. Clothier and Potion Shop create pathways that encourage exploration beyond immediate needs.
  
- **Exploration and Storytelling:** Hidden interactions in the Residential Alley reward players who explore beyond commercial interests. The Potion Shop offers hidden lore and rare items, catering to players' curiosity and sense of discovery.

- **Realism:** The layout reflects a logical organization found in medieval merchant districts, with commercial activities at the forefront and residential areas tucked away, providing a realistic and immersive environment.
