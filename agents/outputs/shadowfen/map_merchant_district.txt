Below is the design for the 'Merchant District' in Shadowfen style. This district is envisioned as a bustling hub of trade and commerce, with a mix of open-air stalls, permanent shops, and residential areas for merchants and their families. The layout encourages exploration and interaction, while maintaining a logical flow.

```
        [Alchemist's Nook]
                |
[Residential Alley] -- [Market Square] -- [Silk Street]
                |                   |
          [Stone Bridge] -- [Riverbank Path]
```

### Room List and Exits:

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits**: North to Residential Alley, East to Silk Street, South to Riverbank Path
   - **Purpose and Connections**: This is the central hub of the Merchant District, where traders and customers converge. It's surrounded by a variety of shops and stalls, making it the liveliest area. The main path to other rooms originates here.

2. **Residential Alley**
   - **Exits**: South to Market Square, North to Alchemist's Nook
   - **Purpose and Connections**: A quieter street lined with modest homes for the district's merchants. It provides a direct path to the Alchemist's Nook, facilitating the flow of both residents and those seeking specialized goods.

3. **Alchemist's Nook**
   - **Exits**: South to Residential Alley
   - **Purpose and Connections**: A secluded shop offering potions and rare ingredients. The location makes it a bit of a secret treasure for those in the know, encouraging exploration beyond the bustling market.

4. **Silk Street**
   - **Exits**: West to Market Square
   - **Purpose and Connections**: Known for its high-end textiles and clothing shops, Silk Street attracts affluent customers. It provides a contrast to the more diverse offerings in the Market Square, creating a natural flow from common goods to luxury items.

5. **Stone Bridge**
   - **Exits**: East to Residential Alley, South to Riverbank Path
   - **Purpose and Connections**: A sturdy stone bridge connecting the residential area with the scenic Riverbank Path. It's a more relaxed route, offering a picturesque view and a transition from the bustling district to the calm riverside.

6. **Riverbank Path**
   - **Exits**: North to Market Square, West to Stone Bridge
   - **Purpose and Connections**: A tranquil path along the river, ideal for a peaceful stroll away from the market's chaos. It serves as a scenic escape and a practical route for those heading to or from the Stone Bridge.

### Design Notes:

- **Flow**: The Market Square acts as the focal point, with easy access to all other rooms, encouraging movement and interaction.
- **Exploration and Discovery**: The Alchemist's Nook is slightly off the beaten path, rewarding those who venture beyond main thoroughfares.
- **Realism and Structure**: The layout is logical for a trade district; commercial areas are centralized, with residential and scenic paths on the periphery.
- **Pacing and Experience**: The district offers a blend of lively commerce and quiet retreats, catering to different player moods and activities.
