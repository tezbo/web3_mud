### Design Notes:

- **Flow:** The Market Square serves as the central hub, allowing players to easily access different shops. Each shop has a logical connection to the central hub, ensuring players can find what they need with ease.
  
- **Discovery:** The Alleyway and Back Room provide opportunities for players to discover hidden content, encouraging exploration beyond the main paths.

- **Purpose:** Each shop is strategically placed around the Market Square to cater to different player needs, from potions to armor to clothing.

- **Pacing:** The layout clusters related activities (shopping) around the Market Square, while offering quieter paths (Alleyway, Side Street) for players seeking something different.

- **Realism:** The spatial organization mimics a realistic market district, with shops centered around a square and accessible from a main gate. This mirrors the typical structure of a bustling medieval market area.
