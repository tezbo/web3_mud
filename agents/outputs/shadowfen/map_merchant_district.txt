### Room List with Exits:
1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to Apothecary, East to Tavern, South to Docks, Up to Watchtower
   - **Notes:** Central hub with high traffic. The square is bustling with vendors, a great place for players to gather information or acquire items.

2. **Apothecary**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Offers potions and herbs. Located north of the Market Square for easy access to supplies. The shadowy interior matches the Shadowfen aesthetic.

3. **Watchtower**
   - **Exits:** Down to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Overlooks the district and provides a vantage point. Accessible via the Market Square, offering a vertical element to the district.

4. **Tavern**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square
   - **Notes:** A place for rest and gathering information. The entrance from the east side provides a natural flow of movement from the market.

5. **Smithy**
   - **Exits:** East to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Provides weapons and armor, essential for adventurers. Located conveniently by the Market Square for quick access to gear.

6. **Docks**
   - **Exits:** North to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Offers a transition to the water routes. Located south, serving as an entry or exit point for those arriving by water, emphasizing the district's trade focus.

### Design Notes:
- **Flow:** The Market Square serves as the central hub, ensuring all paths lead through it, facilitating natural discovery of key locations.
- **Exploration and Convenience:** The Watchtower provides a unique perspective and additional room to explore without cluttering the ground level.
- **Realism and Storytelling:** The placement of the Docks fosters a sense of trade and travel, while the Apothecary and Smithy reflect the district's commercial focus.
- **Vertical Space:** The inclusion of the Watchtower adds vertical interest and a storytelling element of surveillance and protection in a potentially dangerous area.
- **Shadowfen Style:** The district's layout and room purposes reflect the murky, mysterious vibe typical of Shadowfen, with a focus on trade and subtle intrigue.
