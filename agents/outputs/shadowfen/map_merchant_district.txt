**Room List with Exits:**

1. **Guard Post**
   - **Exits:** South to Marketplace
   - **Purpose:** Acts as a checkpoint ensuring safety in the Merchant District. Guards regulate entry, enhancing realism and immersion.

2. **Marketplace**
   - **Exits:** North to Guard Post, East to Potion Shop, West to General Store, South to Tailor
   - **Purpose:** The bustling heart of the Merchant District where traders and adventurers converge. A central hub that provides access to all key services.

3. **Potion Shop**
   - **Exits:** West to Marketplace
   - **Purpose:** A specialized shop offering various potions and ingredients. Its proximity to the Marketplace encourages traffic from adventurers seeking supplies.

4. **General Store**
   - **Exits:** East to Marketplace, South to Blacksmith
   - **Purpose:** Offers a variety of essential goods. Positioned for easy access from the Marketplace and Blacksmith, facilitating resource flow.

5. **Tailor**
   - **Exits:** North to Marketplace
   - **Purpose:** Provides clothing and armor modifications. Located south of the Marketplace for convenient access by those looking to upgrade their attire.

6. **Blacksmith**
   - **Exits:** North to General Store
   - **Purpose:** Supplies weapons and repairs armor. Positioned at the district's edge, creating a sense of journey and specialization. Its location encourages continuation from the General Store.

**Design Notes:**

- **Flow:** The Marketplace serves as the central hub, ensuring all rooms connect logically. This supports player navigation and exploration.
- **Exploration vs. Convenience:** Shops are clustered around the Marketplace for convenience, yet the Blacksmith's position rewards players who venture further in.
- **Realism:** The Guard Post's positioning emphasizes security. The Blacksmith's slight isolation represents the need for space and safety.
- **Storytelling:** The district layout suggests a thriving trade area with a bustling center and specialized services branching out, narrating the story of commerce and community.
- **Pacing:** The layout encourages a natural progression from general to specialized services. The Guard Post sets the stage, while the Blacksmith signifies the district's boundary.
