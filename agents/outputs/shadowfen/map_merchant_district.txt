Below is the design for the 'Merchant District' in Shadowfen style. The district is characterized by its bustling trade, shadowy alleys, and a slightly eerie atmosphere, all set within a swampy region. This district serves as the main commercial hub where players can trade, gather information, and encounter intrigue.

### Room List with Exits and Purpose

1. **Market Square (MS)**
   - **Exits:** North to Alchemist's Lair (AL), East to Armory (AR), South to Trader's Haven (TH)
   - **Purpose:** The central hub of the district, bustling with stalls and merchants. It serves as a convergence point for players looking to buy or sell goods, gather rumors, or find quests.

2. **Alchemist's Lair (AL)**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square (MS), Secret path leading East to Hidden Alleyway (HA)
   - **Purpose:** A dimly-lit shop filled with potions and exotic ingredients. The Alchemist offers unique items and quests. A hidden door leads to the more clandestine parts of the district.

3. **Armory (AR)**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square (MS)
   - **Purpose:** A fortified store stocked with weapons and armor. The Armory is a key location for players to upgrade their combat gear.

4. **Hidden Alleyway (HA)**
   - **Exits:** West to Alchemist's Lair (AL), South to Trader's Haven (TH)
   - **Purpose:** A shadowy, narrow alley that can be used as a shortcut or to evade pursuers. This secretive path is ideal for clandestine meetings and hidden treasures.

5. **Trader's Haven (TH)**
   - **Exits:** North to Market Square (MS), South to Swamp Path (SP), North through Hidden Alleyway (HA)
   - **Purpose:** A more refined trading post where high-value transactions occur. It acts as a quieter alternative to the bustling Market Square.

6. **Swamp Path (SP)**
   - **Exits:** North to Trader's Haven (TH)
   - **Purpose:** A murky trail leading out of the district into the surrounding swamp. It's used for smuggling and access to the swamp's hidden dangers and secrets.

### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose

- **Central Hub:** The Market Square serves as the primary gathering point with easy access to specialized shops and a clear path to hidden areas.
- **Flow and Exploration:** Players are encouraged to explore the Alchemist's Lair for unique items and then discover the Hidden Alleyway, which provides shortcuts and narrative intrigue.
- **Convenient Layout:** Essential services (Armory, Trader's Haven) are clustered around the Market Square, ensuring that players can easily access crucial locations.

- **Vertical Complexity:** While primarily horizontal, the inclusion of secret paths adds depth and rewards exploration.

This design balances the need for a lively, engaging commercial area with opportunities for secretive exploration and narrative development.
