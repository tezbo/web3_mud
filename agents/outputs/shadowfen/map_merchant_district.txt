### Room List with Exits

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to Apothecary, East to Blacksmith, West to General Store
   - **Purpose:** Central hub of the district. Bustling with merchants and customers. The heart of trade and commerce in Shadowfen.

2. **Apothecary**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square, Down to Alchemist's
   - **Purpose:** Specializes in potions and herbs. Offers a mysterious atmosphere with the scent of various concoctions in the air. 

3. **Blacksmith**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square, Down to Armory
   - **Purpose:** Produces and repairs weapons and armor. The constant clanging of metal draws customers and adventurers alike.

4. **General Store**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square, East to Hidden Path
   - **Purpose:** Supplies everyday necessities. A crucial spot for any traveler needing provisions.

5. **Alchemist's**
   - **Exits:** Up to Apothecary
   - **Purpose:** A secretive workshop under the Apothecary, dedicated to more experimental and arcane mixtures. 

6. **Armory**
   - **Exits:** Up to Blacksmith
   - **Purpose:** Houses a collection of completed weapons and armor. A secure area showcasing the Blacksmith's finest wares.

7. **Hidden Path**
   - **Exits:** West to General Store
   - **Purpose:** A discreet shortcut used by those who know about it. It serves as an escape route or a clandestine passage for smuggling goods.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The Market Square acts as the central hub, ensuring players naturally converge here. It directly connects to the key commercial rooms, encouraging exploration from the central point outward.
  
- **Exploration:** The Hidden Path offers a reward for players who explore the less obvious routes. Additionally, the vertical integration of the Alchemist's and Armory rooms adds depth and complexity.
  
- **Purpose and Connections:** Each room is logically placed to reflect its purpose within the district. The Apothecary and Alchemist's are linked, suggesting a layered professional relationship. Similarly, the Blacksmith's and Armory are connected to emphasize production and storage. The General Store's proximity to the Hidden Path adds intrigue and potential narrative hooks.

- **Stylistic Elements:** The Shadowfen style, known for its mysterious and somewhat foreboding atmosphere, is reflected in the ambient details and secretive passages, inviting deeper exploration and engagement with the environment.
