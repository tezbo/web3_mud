### Room List with Exits

1. **Market Square**
   - Exits: North to Blacksmith, East to General Store, South to Tavern
   - Notes: The bustling heart of the Merchant District. Designed as a central hub where players can easily access various shops. The atmosphere is lively, with NPCs showcasing their wares and engaging in trade.

2. **Blacksmith**
   - Exits: South to Market Square
   - Notes: Positioned north of the Market Square to symbolize strength and industry. The blacksmith's forge is a place of constant activity, with clanging sounds of metalwork. Offers weapons and armor.

3. **General Store**
   - Exits: West to Market Square
   - Notes: Located to the east of the Market Square for easy access. Offers a variety of goods and essentials, providing players a convenient stop for common supplies. The store is cluttered yet organized, with shelves piled high with goods.

4. **Tavern**
   - Exits: North to Market Square, South to Tailor Shop
   - Notes: Situated south of the Market Square, the tavern provides a place for players to rest, gather information, and partake in side quests. The ambiance is warm and inviting, with a roaring fireplace and the sound of merry chatter.

5. **Tailor Shop**
   - Exits: North to Tavern, South to Warehouse
   - Notes: South of the Tavern, the tailor shop offers clothing and light armor. It's a quieter space, with the sound of scissors cutting fabric and the soft hum of a sewing machine. A tailored suit or dress can offer social benefits in certain quests.

6. **Warehouse**
   - Exits: North to Tailor Shop
   - Notes: Located at the southern end of the district, the warehouse is a storage area for the district's goods. It serves as a potential quest location, with hidden passages leading to secret areas or illicit dealings. The atmosphere is dusty and dimly lit.

### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose

- **Flow:** The Merchant District is designed with the Market Square as the central hub, facilitating easy navigation between shops. Players organically discover each shop as they explore.
  
- **Purpose:** Each shop is placed logically, complementing the district's role as a commercial hub. The Market Square hosts the main interactions, while the surrounding shops cater to specific needs, enhancing the sense of a thriving trade area.

- **Discovery:** The Warehouse introduces a hint of mystery and potential for exploration with hidden paths, rewarding players who delve deeper.

- **Realism:** The layout mirrors a traditional market district, with shops conveniently clustered around a central square, creating a believable and immersive environment.

- **Pacing:** The sequence from the lively Market Square through to the more secluded Warehouse provides a natural progression from social interaction to potential adventure.
