### Room List and Exits

1. **Market Square**
   - Exits: North to Armory, East to Blacksmith, South to Tavern
   - Design Notes: The bustling heart of the Merchant District, where traders and adventurers converge. It serves as the central hub, providing access to specialized shops. The open space allows for public gatherings and events.

2. **Armory**
   - Exits: South to Market Square
   - Design Notes: Positioned to the north for easy access by city guards and adventurers. Stocks a wide range of weapons and armor. Its proximity to the Market Square encourages impulse buys from passing travelers.

3. **Blacksmith**
   - Exits: West to Market Square
   - Design Notes: Located close to both the Market Square and Armory, ensuring a steady flow of customers. Provides custom weapon and armor crafting, with the sound of hammering metal audible from the square.

4. **Tavern**
   - Exits: North to Market Square, South to Potion Shop, East to Tailor's
   - Design Notes: A social hub connecting the shops, where patrons can rest and share news. Positioned centrally to facilitate easy access from all corners of the district. The tavern serves as a waypoint for players to gather and strategize.

5. **Potion Shop**
   - Exits: North to Tavern
   - Design Notes: Nestled south of the Tavern, offering alchemical supplies and healing potions. Its secluded position creates a sense of discovery, rewarding those who explore beyond the main thoroughfare.

6. **Tailor's**
   - Exits: West to Tavern
   - Design Notes: Conveniently located for adventurers seeking repairs or custom clothing. Positioned to the east of the Tavern to balance the district's layout, providing a quiet retreat from the bustling Market Square.

### Design Notes

- **Flow and Convenience**: The Market Square anchors the district, ensuring that players naturally pass through it. The strategic placement of shops encourages exploration while keeping essential services within easy reach.
- **Exploration and Discovery**: While the main paths are straightforward, the positioning of the Potion Shop rewards players who explore away from the central hub.
- **Realism and Storytelling**: Each shop's location reflects logical city planning, with the Armory and Blacksmith placed for practical access by adventurers and city guards. The Tavern's central position enhances its role as a gathering point.
- **Pacing and Balance**: The layout clusters related services while maintaining a sense of distance and space, encouraging players to traverse the district for specific needs.
