### Merchant District - Shadowfen Style

```
       +-----------+       +----------+
       |  Armory   |-------|  Tailor  |
       |  (Weapons)|       | (Clothes)|
       +-----+-----+       +-----+----+
             |                   |
       +-----+-----+       +-----+----+
       |   Central  |------|  Apothecary|
       |   Market   |      | (Potions)  |
       +-----+-----+       +-----+----+
             |
       +-----+-----+
       |  Stables  |
       | (Mounts)  |
       +-----+-----+
```

### Room List with Exits

1. **Central Market**
   - Exits: North to Armory, Northeast to Tailor, East to Apothecary, South to Stables
   - Purpose: Serves as the central hub of the Merchant District, bustling with activity and vendors selling a variety of goods. It is the main point of orientation for players entering the district.

2. **Armory**
   - Exits: South to Central Market, East to Tailor
   - Purpose: Offers weapons and armor. Positioned close to the market to attract adventurers who prioritize gear. Shares a connection with the Tailor for players interested in both armor and clothing enhancements.

3. **Tailor**
   - Exits: West to Armory, South to Apothecary
   - Purpose: Sells clothing and accessories. Located near the Armory to cater to players interested in crafting and customizing their appearance. The path to the Apothecary encourages exploration of non-combat resources.

4. **Apothecary**
   - Exits: West to Central Market, North to Tailor
   - Purpose: Provides potions and alchemical supplies. Its location ensures easy access for players who need healing or buffs, while its connection to the Tailor suggests a thematic link between potions and crafting.

5. **Stables**
   - Exits: North to Central Market
   - Purpose: Offers mounts and related services. Positioned at the southern edge to symbolize a transition point for players leaving the district, suggesting readiness for travel or exploration beyond the Merchant District.

### Design Notes

- **Flow and Purpose**: The Central Market acts as the nucleus, ensuring players naturally discover key shops. The radial design allows for easy navigation while maintaining a sense of exploration.
  
- **Discovery and Exploration**: The layout encourages players to explore shops that might not be their primary interest, promoting a holistic shopping experience. The Armory and Tailor connection fosters cross-interest exploration.

- **Realism and Storytelling**: Each shop's placement is logical, with related services clustered together. The Stables at the district's edge suggest a logical transition to outdoor areas or other districts.

- **Balance**: The district balances convenience with exploration by offering crucial services near the hub while rewarding players who explore all exits with diverse shopping options.

- **Shadowfen Style**: The layout reflects a compact, efficient district typical of a bustling trade hub in a dense, possibly swampy environment, characteristic of Shadowfen's unique geographical and cultural style.
