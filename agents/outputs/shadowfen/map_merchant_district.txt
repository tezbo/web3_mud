### Room List with Exits

1. **Guild Hall**
   - **Exits:** Down to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** Central hub for traders and merchants, serves as a meeting place and administrative center.

2. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** Up to Guild Hall, East to Armory, South to Apothecary
   - **Purpose:** Central hub of the Merchant District, bustling with stalls and vendors. Main area for players to congregate and trade.

3. **Apothecary**
   - **Exits:** North to Market Square, South to Alchemist Shop
   - **Purpose:** Provides herbs and potions, a vital stop for adventurers needing supplies for healing and enhancements.

4. **Armory**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square, South to Tailor Shop
   - **Purpose:** Sells weapons and armor; essential for players preparing for combat.

5. **Alchemist Shop**
   - **Exits:** North to Apothecary
   - **Purpose:** Specializes in crafting potions and elixirs, offering advanced alchemical services beyond simple apothecary stock.

6. **Tailor Shop**
   - **Exits:** North to Armory
   - **Purpose:** Offers clothing and armor upgrades, customization services for players looking to improve their defense or alter their appearance.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The Market Square serves as the central hub, ensuring high traffic and ease of access to key locations like the Guild Hall, Apothecary, and Armory.
- **Exploration vs. Convenience:** Shops are clustered around the Market Square for convenience, but players must travel through the square to access specialized services, encouraging exploration.
- **Natural Storytelling:** The Guild Hall's position above the Market Square symbolizes its importance and oversight of the district's commerce.
- **Indoor/Outdoor Transitions:** The district is primarily outdoor with covered areas for each shop, enhancing the bustling marketplace atmosphere.
- **Layered Complexity:** While the main paths are straightforward, hidden interactions and NPCs within shops can lead to quests or uncover additional lore about Shadowfen's mercantile traditions.
