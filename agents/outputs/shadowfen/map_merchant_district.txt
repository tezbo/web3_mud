### Room List with Exits

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits**: North to Potion Shop, East to Weapon Shop, South to Residential Quarter
   - **Purpose**: Central hub where merchants gather; lively and bustling with stalls and vendors. Serves as the primary navigation point.

2. **Potion Shop**
   - **Exits**: South to Market Square, Secret passage to Hidden Alley
   - **Purpose**: Sells herbs and potions. Contains a discreet back door leading to the Hidden Alley for those seeking less conventional services.

3. **Weapon Shop**
   - **Exits**: West to Market Square, South to Oddities Shop
   - **Purpose**: Stocks a variety of arms and armor. The shopkeeper has connections to the black market, hinted through interactions.

4. **Hidden Alley**
   - **Exits**: East to Potion Shop (secret), South to Residential Quarter
   - **Purpose**: A narrow, dimly lit alley where secretive deals are made. Accessed via the Potion Shop, offering a sense of discovery for those who explore.

5. **Oddities Shop**
   - **Exits**: North to Weapon Shop, East to Residential Quarter
   - **Purpose**: Offers curios and rare items. The shopkeeper has stories of distant lands, enhancing the mysterious atmosphere.

6. **Residential Quarter**
   - **Exits**: North to Market Square, West to Hidden Alley, South to Oddities Shop
   - **Purpose**: A quieter area with homes and small gardens. Connects to various shops, allowing residents to access goods easily.

### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose

- **Central Hub**: The Market Square serves as a high-traffic central hub, making it easy for players to navigate to various shops and areas.
- **Flow**: The layout encourages players to explore naturally, with the Market Square leading directly to key shops. Hidden Alley offers a reward for those who explore the Potion Shop thoroughly.
- **Discovery**: The secret passage from the Potion Shop to the Hidden Alley introduces an element of surprise and exploration.
- **Pacing**: Shops are clustered around the Market Square for convenience, while the Residential Quarter is slightly more distant, providing a sense of space and quiet.
- **Realism**: The layout makes physical sense, with the central market surrounded by commercial and residential areas.
- **Verticality and Complexity**: While the map is primarily horizontal, the hidden alley adds a layer of complexity and depth. Vertical elements could be incorporated in individual rooms (e.g., Potion Shop has a loft for rare ingredients).

This design allows players to experience the bustling nature of a merchant district while rewarding exploration and creating a sense of mystery through hidden pathways.
