### Room List with Exits

1. **Blacksmith's Forge**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square, East to General Store
   - **Purpose:** Provides armor and weapons. High-traffic area for adventurers preparing for quests.

2. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to Blacksmith's Forge, East to General Store, South to Alchemist's Workshop, West to Apothecary
   - **Purpose:** Central hub of the district. A bustling area where players can find various merchants, street performers, and quest givers.

3. **General Store**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** Supplies general goods and adventuring equipment. Conveniently located for quick access from the market.

4. **Apothecary**
   - **Exits:** South to Alchemist's Workshop, East to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** Sells potions and herbal remedies. Positioned for easy access from the market while providing a quieter corner for players to explore.

5. **Alchemist's Workshop**
   - **Exits:** North to Apothecary, East to Hidden Alley
   - **Purpose:** Offers more specialized alchemical items and crafting opportunities. Acts as a bridge to the Hidden Alley, encouraging exploration.

6. **Hidden Alley**
   - **Exits:** West to Alchemist's Workshop
   - **Purpose:** A secluded area with a mysterious vendor selling rare or illicit goods. Rewards players who explore beyond the obvious paths.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The Market Square serves as the central hub, allowing players to easily navigate to key locations. The cluster of shops around the square promotes natural exploration and interaction.
  
- **Discovery:** The Hidden Alley is an optional area that rewards players who explore the less obvious paths, adding a layer of mystery and intrigue.

- **Pacing:** The arrangement encourages players to visit multiple shops in sequence, with the Alchemistâ€™s Workshop acting as a transition to more secretive areas.

- **Realism:** The layout reflects a logical organization typical of a bustling district, with essential services like the blacksmith and general store directly accessible from the main square.

- **Vertical Space:** While this layout is primarily horizontal, the inclusion of a Hidden Alley suggests verticality in terms of storytelling, as it hints at deeper, possibly subterranean spaces yet to be discovered.
