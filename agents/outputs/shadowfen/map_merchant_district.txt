### Merchant District - Shadowfen Style

```
        [Market Square]
           /   |    \
          /    |     \
[General Store] [Armory] [Apothecary]
          \        |
           \       |
           [Alleyway]
               |
          [Storage Shed]
```

### Room List and Exits

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to General Store, Northeast to Armory, East to Apothecary
   - **Purpose:** Central hub of the Merchant District. Bustling with traders and buyers. A large fountain sits in the center, serving as a meeting point.
   - **Design Notes:** Acts as the main connection point; all other rooms are accessible from here, encouraging players to return.

2. **General Store**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** Supplies everyday goods and essentials. Shelves cramped with items from traveling merchants.
   - **Design Notes:** Positioned to be one of the first stops for players needing basic supplies.

3. **Armory**
   - **Exits:** Southwest to Market Square, South to Alleyway
   - **Purpose:** Offers weapons and armor. The blacksmith's forge can be heard from afar.
   - **Design Notes:** Positioned to allow quick access from the Market Square but also connects to the Alleyway, providing a secretive path.

4. **Apothecary**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** Filled with exotic herbs and potions. A mysterious air permeates the small shop.
   - **Design Notes:** Located at the edge to suggest its slightly mysterious nature, encouraging exploration for curious players.

5. **Alleyway**
   - **Exits:** North to Armory, South to Storage Shed
   - **Purpose:** A narrow path between buildings, often used for discreet transactions.
   - **Design Notes:** Adds a layer of intrigue and a shortcut between Armory and Storage Shed, rewarding players who explore beyond the main paths.

6. **Storage Shed**
   - **Exits:** North to Alleyway
   - **Purpose:** Used to store excess goods for merchants. Occasionally, rare items can be found here.
   - **Design Notes:** Positioned to be a reward location for players who explore the less obvious routes.

### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose

- **Central Hub:** The Market Square serves as the main hub, ensuring high traffic and easy navigation to other key locations.
- **Flow:** Logical placement of shops around the Market Square supports natural flow for players engaging in trade.
- **Discovery:** The Alleyway introduces an element of mystery and serves as a connection to the Storage Shed, rewarding players who explore it.
- **Pacing:** Shops are clustered around the square for convenience, while the Alleyway and Storage Shed provide a slight detour for those seeking additional adventures.
- **Realism:** The layout reflects a realistic merchant district, with shops logically placed around a central gathering area and a hidden alley for discreet activities.
