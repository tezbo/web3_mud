### Room List with Exits:

1. **Guild Square**
   - Exits: South to Market Street
   - Notes: The central hub of the Merchant District, Guild Square is bustling with traders and adventurers. It's a natural gathering place with several notices and job boards, encouraging interaction and commerce.

2. **Market Street**
   - Exits: North to Guild Square, East to Tannery, South to General Store
   - Notes: The main thoroughfare, lined with vendor stalls and shops. It's a vibrant area that connects most of the district's key locations, facilitating smooth flow and exploration.

3. **Tannery**
   - Exits: West to Market Street
   - Notes: A specialized shop for leather goods, located slightly off the main street to manage its pungent odors. It serves as a reminder of the district's industrial side and offers crafting materials.

4. **General Store**
   - Exits: North to Market Street, East to Apothecary, South to Dockside Warehouse
   - Notes: A convenient stop for basic supplies, strategically positioned to serve both travelers from Market Street and those heading towards the docks.

5. **Apothecary**
   - Exits: West to General Store
   - Notes: A small but essential shop providing potions and herbs. Its secluded placement suggests the mysterious nature of its wares and caters to adventurers needing healing supplies.

6. **Dockside Warehouse**
   - Exits: North to General Store
   - Notes: Situated at the southern end, this large storage area is crucial for the import/export activities, reinforcing the district's role in trade. Its proximity to water implies goods are transported via river or sea.

### Design Notes:

- **Flow:** The design encourages players to start at Guild Square and naturally progress through the district. Market Street serves as the main artery, ensuring all rooms are accessible while maintaining a sense of journey.
  
- **Purpose:** Each room has a clear thematic and functional role, from the Guild Square's social hub to the Dockside Warehouse's logistical importance.

- **Exploration vs. Convenience:** While the layout is compact for ease of access, the placement of the Tannery and Apothecary rewards players who explore beyond the main path, offering specialized items.

- **Realism:** The layout reflects a plausible urban design, with industrial, commercial, and logistical spaces logically connected, akin to a bustling medieval trade center.

- **Storytelling:** The district tells a story of a thriving trade hub, with hints of industry and commerce in every corner, encouraging players to engage with its economic ecosystem.
