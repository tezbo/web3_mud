**Room List with Exits:**

1. **Town Square**
   - Exits: North to General Store, South to Potion Shop
   - Notes: The central hub of the Merchant District, bustling with activity. Performers and traders can be found here, attracting players to explore the surrounding shops.

2. **General Store**
   - Exits: South to Town Square, East to Blacksmith
   - Notes: A convenient stop for adventurers to stock up on essentials. Positioned on the main path to encourage visits.

3. **Blacksmith**
   - Exits: West to General Store
   - Notes: Provides weapons and armor. Located adjacent to the General Store for logical resource flow. The clanging of metal can be heard from the Town Square.

4. **Potion Shop**
   - Exits: North to Town Square, East to Tailor
   - Notes: Offers potions and magical items. Positioned opposite the General Store to balance the district and encourage exploration of the southern path.

5. **Tailor**
   - Exits: West to Potion Shop
   - Notes: Sells clothing and accessories. Nestled next to the Potion Shop, it caters to players interested in non-combat gear.

6. **Hidden Alley**
   - Exits: North to Potion Shop
   - Notes: A secretive passageway offering rare and exotic items. Designed as a reward for exploration, encouraging players to search beyond the main paths. The entrance is subtly marked, appealing to keen-eyed adventurers.

**Design Notes:**

- **Flow:** The Town Square acts as the central hub, providing easy access to all shops. The main vertical path allows for straightforward navigation while encouraging players to explore the horizontal connections for a richer experience.

- **Discovery:** The Hidden Alley is an optional, rewarding space for those who venture beyond the obvious paths, adding an element of intrigue and mystery.

- **Realism:** The layout mimics a real-world market district with logical placement of shops and a central gathering place, enhancing immersion.

- **Balance:** The district's design offers a mix of direct paths for convenience and hidden areas for exploration, catering to different player preferences.

- **Storytelling:** Each shop contributes to the narrative of a thriving merchant community within Shadowfen, with the Hidden Alley hinting at the district's secretive and adventurous underbelly.
