3. **Trading Post**
   - Exits: South to Alchemist's Shop
   - Purpose: A place for bulk trading and exchanging goods. Often visited by those seeking to offload large quantities of merchandise.

4. **Tavern**
   - Exits: West to Market Square
   - Purpose: A lively establishment where travelers and locals share stories. Often a source of rumors and quests.

5. **Curio Shop**
   - Exits: North to Alchemist's Shop
   - Purpose: Sells rare and unusual items. A place for those seeking the odd and arcane.

6. **Smuggler's Alley**
   - Exits: North to Market Square
   - Purpose: A hidden, less reputable area where under-the-table deals occur. Offers connections to the black market.
