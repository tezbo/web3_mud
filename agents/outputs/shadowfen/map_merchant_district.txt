Below is the design for the "Merchant District" in Shadowfen style, featuring a central hub, logical organization, and exploration opportunities.

```
       [Alchemy Shop]
            |
       [Central Plaza]---[General Store]
            |      \
       [Caravan Office] [Weapon Smith]
            |
       [Hidden Alley]
```

### Room List with Exits:

1. **Central Plaza**
   - Exits: North to Alchemy Shop, East to General Store, Southeast to Weapon Smith, South to Caravan Office
   - Notes: The heart of the Merchant District, it serves as a high-traffic hub. Players naturally congregate here, discovering other locations through exploration.

2. **Alchemy Shop**
   - Exits: South to Central Plaza
   - Notes: Specializes in potions and magical ingredients. Positioned conveniently north of the Plaza, it encourages players to explore further into the district.

3. **General Store**
   - Exits: West to Central Plaza
   - Notes: Offers a variety of goods for adventurers. Located to the east of the Plaza for easy access, acting as a staple for player needs.

4. **Weapon Smith**
   - Exits: Northwest to Central Plaza
   - Notes: Provides weapons and armor. Slightly tucked away, it requires players to venture southeast from the Plaza, rewarding exploration with essential gear.

5. **Caravan Office**
   - Exits: North to Central Plaza, South to Hidden Alley
   - Notes: Facilitates trade and travel arrangements. Positioned south of the Plaza, it acts as a transition to less obvious paths, hinting at the Hidden Alley.

6. **Hidden Alley**
   - Exits: North to Caravan Office
   - Notes: A secluded path that rewards curious players. May contain secretive vendors or rare items. Its discreet location encourages exploration and discovery.

### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose:

- **Central Hub:** The Central Plaza serves as a natural gathering point, with multiple exits leading to various merchant locations.
- **Flow:** The district is designed to guide players naturally from the Plaza to essential shops like the General Store and Alchemy Shop while providing a sense of discovery towards the Weapon Smith and Hidden Alley.
- **Exploration:** The Hidden Alley offers a secretive area that rewards players who stray from the main path, enhancing the sense of adventure and discovery.
- **Purpose:** Each shop is logically placed to represent a bustling merchant hub, with the Central Plaza as the focal point, ensuring easy navigation and thematic consistency with the Shadowfen style.
