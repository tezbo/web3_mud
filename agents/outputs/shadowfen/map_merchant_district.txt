### Merchant District in Shadowfen Style

```
   [Alchemy Shop] -- [Blacksmith]
         |                 |
   [General Store] -- [Market Square] -- [Tavern]
         |                 |
  [Residential Alley] -- [Tailor]
```

#### Room List with Exits:

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits**: North (Alchemy Shop), East (Tavern), South (General Store), West (Blacksmith)
   - **Purpose**: Central hub of the Merchant District; a bustling area where various paths converge.

2. **Alchemy Shop**
   - **Exits**: South (Market Square), East (Blacksmith)
   - **Purpose**: Offers potions and magical ingredients; provides a quieter atmosphere away from the main hustle.

3. **Blacksmith**
   - **Exits**: East (Market Square), West (Alchemy Shop)
   - **Purpose**: Sells weapons and armor; the clang of metal can be heard, attracting adventurers.

4. **General Store**
   - **Exits**: North (Market Square), South (Residential Alley)
   - **Purpose**: Supplies everyday items; acts as a transition from the commercial area to the residential zone.

5. **Tavern**
   - **Exits**: West (Market Square)
   - **Purpose**: A place of rest and information; offers quests and social interaction.

6. **Residential Alley**
   - **Exits**: North (General Store), East (Tailor)
   - **Purpose**: A narrow passage lined with homes; a quiet area leading to specialized shops.

7. **Tailor**
   - **Exits**: West (Residential Alley)
   - **Purpose**: Sells clothing and armor; a quieter shop tucked away from the main paths.

#### Design Notes:

- **Flow**: The Market Square serves as the central hub, allowing players to naturally navigate to key locations. Shops are clustered around the square, ensuring easy access to essential services.
- **Exploration**: The Residential Alley introduces a sense of discovery as players move away from the bustling market to quieter, specialized areas like the Tailor.
- **Convenience vs. Exploration**: The direct connections to the Market Square offer convenience, while the side path through the Residential Alley rewards players who explore with a discovery of the Tailor.
- **Realism**: The layout mimics a realistic town setup with a central market and surrounding shops, transitioning to quieter residential areas.
- **Pacing**: The bustling Market Square contrasts with the more serene Residential Alley and Alchemy Shop, providing varied experiences for players.
