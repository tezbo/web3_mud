#### Room List with Exits:

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to Herbalist's Hut, West to Blacksmith, East to General Store, South to Tailor's Shop
   - **Purpose:** Central hub of the district where players can interact with various NPCs, purchase goods from different stalls, and gather information.

2. **Tailor's Shop**
   - **Exits:** North to Market Square, South to Alleyway
   - **Purpose:** A specialized shop for purchasing clothing and fabrics. Offers custom tailoring services. Serves as an entry point to the hidden alleyway.

3. **General Store**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** Provides a variety of general supplies and goods. A convenient stop for adventurers to stock up on essentials before their journeys.

4. **Blacksmith**
   - **Exits:** East to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** Sells weapons and tools. Offers repair services. Creates a sense of realism by being located near the central hub, where demand for such services is high.

5. **Herbalist's Hut**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square
   - **Purpose:** A quieter, more secluded location selling herbs and potions. Offers unique items not found in the general store. Encourages exploration to find special goods.

6. **Alleyway**
   - **Exits:** North to Tailor's Shop
   - **Purpose:** A hidden path leading to the sewers, providing an opportunity for exploration and discovery. Offers a shortcut or secret entrance to other parts of the Shadowfen.

#### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose:

- **Central Hub:** The Market Square serves as the bustling center of the Merchant District, naturally leading players to the various specialized shops.
- **Logical Layout:** Shops are logically arranged around the Market Square, ensuring easy navigation while maintaining a sense of distance and exploration.
- **Discovery Elements:** The hidden Alleyway leading to the sewers adds an element of mystery and encourages players to explore.
- **Thematic Consistency:** The Shadowfen style is maintained with atmospheric shops like the Herbalist's Hut, which adds to the immersive experience.
- **Balanced Exploration:** While the district is compact, the arrangement of rooms and the inclusion of a hidden path offer both convenience and the reward of exploration.
