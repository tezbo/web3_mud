### Room List

1. **Auction House**
   - **Exits**: South to Marketplace
   - **Notes**: Central hub for trading and auctioning goods. Players can gather information, participate in auctions, and trade with others. It's a key social and economic center, leading directly to the bustling Marketplace.

2. **Marketplace**
   - **Exits**: North to Auction House, West to Alchemist, East to Tailor, South to Cobbler
   - **Notes**: The vibrant heart of the Merchant District with stalls and vendors selling a variety of goods. It serves as the main connector to other specialized shops, encouraging exploration and interaction.

3. **Alchemist**
   - **Exits**: East to Marketplace
   - **Notes**: A mysterious, aromatic shop filled with potions and reagents. Itâ€™s slightly tucked away, offering a sense of discovery for those exploring the western paths from the Marketplace. Ideal for players seeking magical or alchemical supplies.

4. **Cobbler**
   - **Exits**: North to Marketplace
   - **Notes**: A quaint shop where leather goods and footwear are crafted. Positioned to draw players further south from the Marketplace, balancing the flow by extending the district's reach.

5. **Tailor**
   - **Exits**: West to Marketplace
   - **Notes**: A brightly decorated shop offering textiles and garments. Situated to encourage players to explore the eastern side of the Marketplace, maintaining a balanced spatial design with its counterpart, the Alchemist.

### Design Notes

- **Flow**: The Marketplace acts as a central hub, allowing players to naturally flow between specialized shops. The Auction House's strategic placement north of the Marketplace draws players into the district.
- **Discovery**: By situating the Alchemist slightly to the west, it encourages exploration beyond the central path. The arrangement avoids linear movement, promoting a dynamic exploration experience.
- **Pacing**: Shops are clustered around the Marketplace, ensuring related services are conveniently accessible while retaining a sense of distance and exploration.
- **Realism**: The layout reflects a believable town structure, where common trade areas are central, and specialized services are slightly off the main path. This mirrors the logical organization of a bustling merchant district.

By integrating these design principles, the Merchant District offers a balanced mix of convenience and exploration, encouraging players to engage with both the central hub and peripheral areas within the district.
