**Room List with Exits:**

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to Auction House, East to Weapon Shop, West to Potion Shop, South to Tailor Shop
   - **Notes:** Central hub of the Merchant District. Bustling with vendors and customers. Main gathering and trade point.

2. **Auction House**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Place for high-value goods and rare items. Attracts wealthier clientele. Elevated space to signify importance.

3. **Potion Shop**
   - **Exits:** East to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Offers various potions and elixirs. Cozy and mysterious, with shelves lined with colorful bottles.

4. **Weapon Shop**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Sells weapons and armor. Clanging of metal often heard. Display of craftsmanship attracts adventurers.

5. **Tailor Shop**
   - **Exits:** North to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Provides custom clothing and armor fittings. Known for exquisite fabrics and tailoring skills.

**Design Notes:**

- **Central Hub:** Market Square serves as the hub, ensuring all shops are easily accessible but still promoting exploration as players have to navigate through the square.
- **Flow:** Players entering the district naturally arrive at the Market Square, encouraging exploration of surrounding shops.
- **Discovery:** Potential for hidden vendors or side quests within the Auction House or Tailor Shop, rewarding keen explorers.
- **Pacing:** Logical clustering of related shops around the central square balances exploration and convenience.
- **Realism:** Layout mimics a real-world marketplace style, with the Auction House being a focal point for high-profile transactions.
- **Vertical Space:** Not utilized in this district, keeping it straightforward but with potential for future vertical expansions (e.g., upper floors in shops).
