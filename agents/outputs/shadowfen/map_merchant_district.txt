### Room List with Exits:

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to [Armory], Northeast to [General Store]
   - **Purpose:** Central hub of the Merchant District. A bustling open area where merchants set up stalls. The square is filled with sounds of haggling and the scent of exotic spices.

2. **Armory**
   - **Exits:** South to [Market Square], South to [Tailor]
   - **Purpose:** Specializes in weapons and armor. Positioned near the Market Square for easy access to adventurers. Has a sturdy, fortified appearance.

3. **General Store**
   - **Exits:** Southwest to [Market Square], South to [Alchemist]
   - **Purpose:** Sells a variety of goods including adventuring supplies and everyday items. Positioned for convenience, attracting both locals and travelers.

4. **Tailor**
   - **Exits:** North to [Armory], East to [Alchemist], South to [Warehouse]
   - **Purpose:** Offers bespoke clothing and textiles. Located between the Armory and Warehouse, serving both craftsmen and traders.

5. **Alchemist**
   - **Exits:** North to [General Store], West to [Tailor]
   - **Purpose:** Sells potions, remedies, and magical ingredients. A slightly secluded shop, providing a quieter atmosphere for potion brewing.

6. **Warehouse**
   - **Exits:** North to [Tailor]
   - **Purpose:** Storage for goods and supplies. Positioned at the district's edge, facilitating easy loading and unloading of merchandise.

### Design Notes:

- **Flow:** The Market Square serves as the main hub, with shops radiating outwards to ensure easy navigation for players. The Armory and General Store are directly accessible from the square, catering to different player needs.
- **Discovery:** Players exploring beyond the Market Square will find interconnected shops like the Tailor and Alchemist, rewarding them with specialized goods.
- **Pacing:** Related shops like the Tailor and Armory are closely grouped to provide logical shopping trips. The Warehouse is a bit further, emphasizing its purpose as a storage facility.
- **Realism:** The layout reflects a thriving commercial area, with the Market Square as the focal point. The arrangement of shops supports a logical flow of trade and goods.
- **Vertical Space:** While not utilized in this district, the potential for vertical expansion exists, such as adding a second-floor tailoring workshop or a basement storage in the warehouse.
