### Room List with Exits and Purpose

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** East to [2], South to [4]
   - **Purpose:** Central hub of the Merchant District. This bustling square is filled with stalls and vendors, offering a variety of goods. It acts as a gathering place and the main point of entry for visitors.

2. **Potion Shop**
   - **Exits:** West to [1], East to [3], South to [5]
   - **Purpose:** A small, dimly lit shop filled with shelves of colorful potions. The shopkeeper is knowledgeable about remedies and poisons alike. This shop stands as a vital resource for adventurers.

3. **Armorer's Forge**
   - **Exits:** West to [2], South to [6]
   - **Purpose:** The clang of metal rings from this forge. The armorer specializes in crafting and repairing weapons and armor. It is a critical stop for those preparing for battles.

4. **Tavern**
   - **Exits:** North to [1], East to [5]
   - **Purpose:** A lively place where locals and travelers alike share stories and rest. The tavern offers a warm meal and a place to gather information or hire a guide. It's directly accessible from the Market Square to encourage social interaction.

5. **General Store**
   - **Exits:** North to [2], West to [4], East to [6]
   - **Purpose:** This store sells a bit of everything, from travel supplies to everyday items. It bridges the gap between the potion shop and armorer's forge, making it a convenient stop for a variety of needs.

6. **Black Market**
   - **Exits:** North to [3], West to [5]
   - **Purpose:** Hidden from casual eyes, this clandestine market deals in rare and illicit goods. It's a place for those in the know, requiring exploration to discover its location.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The central Market Square [1] serves as the primary hub, allowing players to naturally flow into specialized shops [2, 3] and community spaces like the Tavern [4].
- **Discovery:** The Black Market [6] requires exploration, rewarding players who venture through the district.
- **Pacing:** Shops are clustered around the central square, with related establishments (e.g., Potion Shop and Armorer's Forge) nearby.
- **Realism:** The layout mimics a logical merchant district, where related businesses are clustered for convenience, while secretive operations like the Black Market are tucked away.
- **Vertical Space:** Although not used here, opportunities for vertical exploration could include hidden basements or upper shop levels for future expansion.
