### Room List and Exits

1. **Street Corner**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Entry point into the Merchant District. A bustling intersection where travelers arrive, with signposts directing to the Market Square. It sets the stage for the district's vibrant energy.

2. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to Street Corner, West to Curio Shop, East to Tavern, South to Bazaar
   - **Notes:** Central hub of the Merchant District. A lively area filled with vendors. Acts as the main traffic conduit connecting various shops and providing a sense of community and commerce.

3. **Curio Shop**
   - **Exits:** East to Market Square, South to Alleyway
   - **Notes:** A quaint shop filled with exotic and mysterious items. Offers a hidden passage to the Alleyway, rewarding those who explore. Emphasizes the district's eclectic nature.

4. **Bazaar**
   - **Exits:** North to Market Square, South to Alleyway
   - **Notes:** A sprawling outdoor market with numerous stalls selling everything from local crafts to rare ingredients. The Bazaar is a key area for player interactions and trades.

5. **Tavern**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square, South to Alleyway
   - **Notes:** A popular gathering place for adventurers and locals alike. Provides information, quests, and a chance to rest. Its proximity to the Market Square makes it a convenient meeting point.

6. **Alleyway**
   - **Exits:** North to Curio Shop, North to Bazaar, North to Tavern
   - **Notes:** A narrow, shadowy path connecting the district's main areas. Offers shortcuts between the Curio Shop, Bazaar, and Tavern, rewarding players familiar with the area and providing a sense of layered exploration.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The Market Square serves as the central hub, ensuring all major locations are easily accessible while maintaining the district's bustling atmosphere. Players naturally discover shops and the tavern through this design.
  
- **Exploration and Discovery:** The Alleyway provides hidden paths and shortcuts, encouraging players to explore off the beaten path for quicker navigation and discovery of secrets.

- **Pacing:** The district is compact with a logical organization, clustering shops around the Market Square for ease of access while retaining a sense of exploration through the Alleyway and Curio Shop's hidden passage.

- **Realism:** The layout makes physical sense, with the Market Square acting as a central access point, surrounded by relevant commercial and social spaces, typical of a vibrant merchant district.
