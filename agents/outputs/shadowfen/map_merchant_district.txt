- **Discovery:** The Hidden Alley adds a layer of intrigue, accessible only from the Blacksmith and Tailor. It rewards players who explore beyond the obvious paths, providing shortcuts and hidden opportunities.
  
- **Realism:** The Merchant District's layout reflects realistic urban planning, with the central market surrounded by specialized shops. The Hidden Alley is a believable feature, offering both a sense of mystery and practical routing.

- **Pacing:** The rooms are clustered with related shops close to each other, while the Hidden Alley introduces a more secluded, optional area for players seeking adventure or secrecy.

This layout balances exploration with convenience, ensuring that players can easily navigate the district while also encountering rewarding discoveries.
