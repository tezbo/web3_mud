### Room List with Exits and Design Notes

1. **Market Square**
   - **Exits:** North to Blacksmith, West to Tavern, East to Alchemist, South to Tailor
   - **Notes:** The central hub of the Merchant District, bustling with activity. It serves as the primary navigation point, facilitating commerce and social interaction. The square is alive with vendors, and players will naturally be drawn here as they explore the district.

2. **Blacksmith**
   - **Exits:** South to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Located north of the Market Square, the Blacksmith is where players can craft and repair equipment. Positioned strategically to encourage players to pass through the Market Square, reinforcing its role as the district's heart.

3. **Tavern**
   - **Exits:** East to Market Square, South to Hidden Path
   - **Notes:** A place for rest and gathering information, the Tavern is directly accessible from the Market Square. A hidden path in the tavern's cellar leads to secretive areas, rewarding curious players.

4. **Alchemist**
   - **Exits:** West to Market Square, South to Hidden Path
   - **Notes:** The Alchemist's shop, nestled east of the Market Square, offers potions and mystical items. A hidden path behind a shelf connects to the Tavern's cellar, providing an alternate route for those who explore.

5. **Tailor**
   - **Exits:** North to Market Square
   - **Notes:** Situated south of the Market Square, the tailor's shop is where players can purchase and customize clothing. Its location encourages a full circuit around the Market for players traveling between shops.

6. **Hidden Path**
   - **Exits:** North to Tavern, East to Alchemist
   - **Notes:** A secret passage connecting the Tavern and Alchemist, encouraging exploration and discovery. This path adds depth and intrigue, offering shortcuts and potential hidden quests or items.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The Market Square serves as the central hub, with all major shops radiating from it. This design ensures players naturally encounter important locations while exploring.
- **Discovery:** The hidden path linking the Tavern and Alchemist adds an element of secrecy, rewarding players who explore thoroughly.
- **Pacing:** The district is compact yet offers enough variety in its layout to keep players engaged. The hidden path and varied shop locations maintain interest.
- **Realism:** The layout reflects a realistic small merchant district, with related shops positioned logically around a central square.
- **Vertical Space:** Although the district itself is on one level, vertical exploration is implied in the tavern (cellar leading to the hidden path).

This design balances exploration with convenience, creating a lively and engaging Merchant District in the Shadowfen style.
