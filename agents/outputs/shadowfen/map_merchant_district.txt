**Merchant District - Shadowfen Style**

```
            [Market Square]
                 |
   [Apothecary]<-[Main Street]->[General Store]
                 |
            [Tailor Shop]
                 |
            [Blacksmith]
                 |
            [Hidden Alley]
```

**Room List with Exits:**

1. **Market Square**
   - Exits: North to Main Street
   - Notes: The bustling heart of the Merchant District, where traders set up stalls. High-traffic area with vibrant activity. Serves as the central hub for navigation within the district.

2. **Main Street**
   - Exits: South to Market Square, West to Apothecary, East to General Store, South to Tailor Shop
   - Notes: The primary thoroughfare lined with various shops. Encourages exploration with direct access to key merchant locations.

3. **Apothecary**
   - Exits: East to Main Street
   - Notes: Specializes in potions and herbal remedies. Located on the quieter side of Main Street, offering respite from the bustling market. 

4. **General Store**
   - Exits: West to Main Street
   - Notes: Supplies adventurers with general goods. Positioned for convenience, drawing in both locals and travelers from the Market Square.

5. **Tailor Shop**
   - Exits: North to Main Street, South to Blacksmith
   - Notes: Offers custom clothing and armor. Connects the northern commercial area with the more industrial southern end.

6. **Blacksmith**
   - Exits: North to Tailor Shop, South to Hidden Alley
   - Notes: The district's industrial hub, providing crafted weapons and tools. The rhythmic sound of hammer on anvil can be heard from afar.

7. **Hidden Alley**
   - Exits: North to Blacksmith
   - Notes: A secretive path known to few, rumored to be a shortcut to an undisclosed location. Rewards players who take the time to explore beyond the obvious paths.

**Design Notes:**

- **Flow & Exploration:** The district encourages exploration with a central hub (Market Square) and a main street branching out to key locations. The presence of a hidden alley adds an element of mystery.
  
- **Convenience & Realism:** Shops are logically placed with a central market and surrounding specialized stores, mimicking a realistic medieval trading district.
  
- **Pacing & Discovery:** The district is compact yet layered, with hidden elements (Hidden Alley) providing depth for players looking to uncover secrets.
  
- **Natural Storytelling:** The layout tells a story of a thriving mercantile area, from the bustling heart of the Market Square to the industrious Blacksmith, offering insights into the district's economic life.
