### Room List and Exits:

2. **Potion Shop**
   - Exits: South to Marketplace, West to Blacksmith
   - Notes: A compact shop filled with shelves of potions and ingredients. Positioned close to the marketplace for easy access to alchemical supplies.

3. **Blacksmith**
   - Exits: East to Potion Shop
   - Notes: The clanging of metal echoes through the shop. Situated near the Potion Shop for logical synergy between crafting and enchanting tools/weapons.

4. **General Store**
   - Exits: North to Marketplace, South to Alchemist's Alley
   - Notes: A catch-all store offering a variety of goods. Located just south of the Marketplace for convenience, serving as an introduction to the more specialized shops.

5. **Alchemist's Alley**
   - Exits: East to General Store, West to Hidden Courtyard

   - Exits: East to Alchemist's Alley
   - Notes: A secluded area with a small fountain and benches, offering a peaceful retreat. Provides a hidden reward for players who explore beyond the typical routes.

### Design Notes:

- **Flow and Exploration:** The Marketplace serves as a central hub, with logical paths leading to each specialized shop. The design encourages movement through the district while allowing players to discover hidden areas like the Hidden Courtyard.
- **Purpose and Connection:** Shops are organized to reflect practical adjacency (e.g., Potion Shop near Blacksmith). This spatial arrangement improves player immersion and storytelling.
- **Discovery and Convenience:** The Alchemist's Alley and Hidden Courtyard provide opportunities for players to find unique items, rewarding those who venture beyond the main thoroughfare.
- **Realism and Style:** The layout mimics a realistic district with a central marketplace, specialized shops, and hidden paths, fitting the Shadowfen's mysterious and bustling atmosphere.
