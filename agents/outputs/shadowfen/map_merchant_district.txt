### Room List with Exits and Design Notes

1. **Town Square**
   - **Exits:** North to General Goods Store, South to Shadowfen Tavern, East to Residential Area (not mapped)
   - **Design Notes:** Central hub of the Merchant District. A bustling area with street vendors and performers, serving as the primary navigation point. Encourages exploration by providing multiple pathways.

2. **Blacksmith's Forge**
   - **Exits:** North to General Goods Store, South to Town Square
   - **Design Notes:** Conveniently located close to the Town Square. The rhythmic clanging of metal attracts players. Provides essential services and equipment for adventurers.

3. **General Goods Store**
   - **Exits:** South to Blacksmith's Forge, East to Alchemist's Shop
   - **Design Notes:** Offers a wide variety of items for purchase. Positioned between the Blacksmith and the Alchemist, forming a corridor of commerce. Encourages players to explore the full range of available goods.

4. **Alchemist's Shop**
   - **Exits:** West to General Goods Store
   - **Design Notes:** A mysterious shop filled with potions and rare ingredients. Located off the main path, encouraging curious players to delve deeper. Adds an element of discovery with unique items.

5. **Shadowfen Tavern**
   - **Exits:** North to Town Square, East to Secret Cellar
   - **Design Notes:** A social gathering spot for adventurers and locals. Offers rest, rumors, and quests. The tavern's lively atmosphere contrasts with the secretive nature of the cellar below.

6. **Secret Cellar**
   - **Exits:** Up to Shadowfen Tavern
   - **Design Notes:** Hidden beneath the tavern, accessed via a trapdoor. A perfect spot for clandestine meetings or hidden treasures. Adds vertical complexity and an element of mystery to the district.

### Design Summary

The Merchant District is designed to balance exploration with convenience, encouraging players to navigate through a network of shops and services. The Town Square acts as the central hub, ensuring smooth flow while promoting discovery. Vertical space in the form of the Secret Cellar enhances the district's complexity, rewarding players who seek out hidden paths. This layout maintains realism and logical organization, creating an immersive and engaging experience in the Shadowfen style.
