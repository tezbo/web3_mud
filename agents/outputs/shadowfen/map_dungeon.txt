Room List with Exits:

1. **Entrance**
   - Exits: North to Main Hall
   - Purpose: Starting point of the dungeon, setting the mood with damp, mossy walls and flickering torches. Acts as a buffer between the outside world and the depths of the dungeon.

2. **Main Hall**
   - Exits: South to Entrance, North to Hidden Tunnel, East to Prison Cell
   - Purpose: Central hub of the dungeon, a large, echoing chamber with crumbling stone pillars. It provides access to other areas, encouraging players to explore further.

3. **Prison Cell**
   - Exits: West to Main Hall
   - Purpose: A grim reminder of the dungeon's dark history, this room contains rusted iron bars and old shackles. It's a dead-end but offers clues or items relevant to the dungeon's lore.

4. **Hidden Tunnel**
   - Exits: South to Main Hall, East to Secret Chamber, North to Treasure Vault
   - Purpose: A narrow, winding passage that rewards keen-eyed explorers. The tunnel is partially obscured by shadows and overgrown roots, suggesting its hidden nature.

5. **Secret Chamber**
   - Exits: West to Hidden Tunnel
   - Purpose: A secluded room filled with mysterious artifacts and faint magical energies. This optional area provides story elements and minor treasures, encouraging exploration.

6. **Treasure Vault**
   - Exits: South to Hidden Tunnel
   - Purpose: The final reward for those who venture deep into the dungeon. It contains a trove of valuables protected by ancient traps, serving as the ultimate goal for treasure-seekers.

Design Notes on Flow and Purpose:
- The Entrance serves as a threshold that sets the stage for the entire dungeon, providing a sense of foreboding and anticipation.
- The Main Hall acts as the central hub, with multiple exits leading to other areas, promoting a non-linear exploration experience.
- The Prison Cell offers a narrative element and a sense of history, emphasizing the dungeon's past usage.
- The Hidden Tunnel introduces an element of discovery, rewarding players who pay attention to their surroundings with access to the Secret Chamber and Treasure Vault.
- The Secret Chamber provides additional lore and minor rewards, enriching the dungeon's story.
- The Treasure Vault serves as the climax, offering substantial rewards and a sense of accomplishment for players who navigate the dungeon's challenges.
