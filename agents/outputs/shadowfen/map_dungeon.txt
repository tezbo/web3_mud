### ASCII Map of the 'Dungeon' District in Shadowfen Style

```
      [Guard Post]
          |
    [Prison Cells 1]--[Prison Cells 2]
          |
      [Main Chamber]
          |
      [Torture Room]--[Hidden Armory]
```

### Room List with Exits

1. **Guard Post**
   - **Exits**: South to Main Chamber
   - **Notes**: Entry point to the dungeon, where guards monitor the entrance. This room serves as a checkpoint and security measure, ensuring that only authorized personnel proceed further into the dungeon.

2. **Prison Cells 1**
   - **Exits**: South to Main Chamber, East to Prison Cells 2
   - **Notes**: The first cluster of prison cells, housing various prisoners. A direct connection to the Main Chamber allows for easy access to move prisoners as needed.

3. **Prison Cells 2**
   - **Exits**: West to Prison Cells 1
   - **Notes**: Additional cells for overflow or higher-security prisoners. This area is more isolated to increase security for dangerous captives.

4. **Main Chamber**
   - **Exits**: North to Guard Post, South to Torture Room, East to Prison Cells 1
   - **Notes**: Central hub of the dungeon, facilitating movement between the guard post, cells, and torture room. It serves as an observation area for the guards and dungeon master.

5. **Torture Room**
   - **Exits**: North to Main Chamber, East to Hidden Armory
   - **Notes**: A grim area used for interrogation and punishment. Linked directly to the Main Chamber for easy movement of prisoners and personnel.

6. **Hidden Armory**
   - **Exits**: West to Torture Room
   - **Notes**: A secretive location accessible from the Torture Room, used to store weapons and equipment. Its hidden nature provides strategic advantages during emergencies.

### Design Notes

- **Flow and Purpose**: The dungeon's design emphasizes security and control. The Guard Post acts as the initial barrier, while the Main Chamber serves as the central hub, allowing for efficient oversight and management of the dungeon's various areas.
- **Exploration vs. Convenience**: The layout encourages exploration, with the Hidden Armory rewarding those who investigate the Torture Room closely. The central Main Chamber ensures convenient access to key areas while maintaining a sense of distance and scale.
- **Realism and Storytelling**: The clustering of prison cells near the Main Chamber reflects a logical organization for managing prisoners, while the hidden nature of the armory provides narrative depth, suggesting secrets and hidden agendas within the dungeon's operations.
- **Vertical Space and Complexity**: Though a single level, the dungeon's design incorporates complexity through hidden paths and strategic room placement, enhancing the player's sense of discovery and immersion in the Shadowfen atmosphere.
