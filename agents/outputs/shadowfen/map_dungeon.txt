### Room List with Exits

1. **Entrance Hall**
   - **Exits:** South to [Central Hub]
   - **Notes:** The starting point for adventurers, featuring ancient murals that hint at the dungeon's history.

2. **Central Hub**
   - **Exits:** North to [Entrance Hall], East to [Guard Room], West to [Trap Room], and Secret Passage to [Hidden Chamber]
   - **Notes:** The heart of the dungeon, a large chamber that acts as a crossroad. It contains a stone pedestal with markings that point to various paths.

3. **Guard Room**
   - **Exits:** West to [Central Hub]
   - **Notes:** Once the quarters for dungeon sentries, now inhabited by spectral guards. Contains clues about the dungeon's defenses.

4. **Trap Room**
   - **Exits:** East to [Central Hub], North to [Hidden Chamber]
   - **Notes:** A perilous corridor filled with mechanical traps. Successful navigation requires solving a puzzle related to the murals in the Entrance Hall.

5. **Hidden Chamber**
   - **Exits:** South to [Trap Room], Secret Passage to [Treasure Vault], Secret Passage to [Central Hub]
   - **Notes:** A concealed area accessed via a hidden mechanism in the Central Hub. Contains magical artifacts and lore about the dungeon's creation.

6. **Treasure Vault**
   - **Exits:** South to [Hidden Chamber]
   - **Notes:** The final reward for diligent explorers, housing the dungeon's legendary treasures. The vault is guarded by a powerful entity that tests the player's virtue.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The Central Hub serves as the main navigation point, providing access to various parts of the dungeon. Players are naturally drawn towards it after entering from the Entrance Hall.
  
- **Exploration & Discovery:** Hidden passages from the Central Hub and the Hidden Chamber reward players who explore off the beaten path. Clues in the Entrance Hall murals and the Guard Room enhance immersion and storytelling.

- **Purpose & Pacing:** The Entrance Hall introduces players to the dungeon's backstory. The Guard Room and Trap Room present challenges that must be overcome to access the Hidden Chamber. The Treasure Vault acts as the ultimate goal, providing a satisfying conclusion to the journey.

- **Realism & Style:** The dungeon layout reflects a logical progression from entrance to treasure, with thematic elements consistent with Shadowfen's mysterious and ancient atmosphere.

This design balances exploration with a clear progression path, encouraging players to engage with both the narrative and the environment.
