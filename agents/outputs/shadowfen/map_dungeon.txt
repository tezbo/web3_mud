### Shadowfen Dungeon Map

```
       [Guard Post]
            |
       [Main Hall] -- [Prison Cells]
            |
       [Torture Chamber]
            |
      +-- [Armory] --+
      |              |
[Secret Passage]  [Treasure Room]
```

### Room List with Exits

1. **Guard Post**
   - **Exits:** South to Main Hall
   - **Purpose:** Entry point with guards stationed to monitor access to the dungeon.
   - **Notes:** Sets the mood with tense atmosphere; a checkpoint for unauthorized entry.

2. **Main Hall**
   - **Exits:** North to Guard Post, East to Prison Cells, South to Torture Chamber
   - **Purpose:** Central hub connecting key areas of the dungeon.
   - **Notes:** Designed for high traffic, with a foreboding ambiance. The focal point for navigation within the dungeon.

3. **Prison Cells**
   - **Exits:** West to Main Hall
   - **Purpose:** Containment area for prisoners.
   - **Notes:** Provides storytelling opportunities with NPC interactions; hints at the dungeon's sinister nature.

4. **Torture Chamber**
   - **Exits:** North to Main Hall, South to Armory
   - **Purpose:** Interrogation and punishment room.
   - **Notes:** Dark theme with grisly details; adds depth to the dungeon's narrative.

5. **Armory**
   - **Exits:** North to Torture Chamber, East to Treasure Room, West to Secret Passage
   - **Purpose:** Storage for weapons and armor; strategic supply location.
   - **Notes:** Key for combat preparation; hidden path access enhances exploration.

6. **Secret Passage**
   - **Exits:** East to Armory
   - **Purpose:** Concealed route for stealthy navigation.
   - **Notes:** Encourages exploration; rewards players with shortcuts and surprises.

7. **Treasure Room**
   - **Exits:** West to Armory
   - **Purpose:** Vault for valuables and loot.
   - **Notes:** Final reward for exploration; contains traps for added challenge.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The dungeon is designed with a central hub (Main Hall) that branches into key areas, encouraging exploration while maintaining logical connections.
- **Purpose:** Each room serves a distinct role within the dungeon, contributing to the overarching theme of imprisonment and secrecy.
- **Discovery:** Hidden paths like the Secret Passage offer players the chance to uncover shortcuts and gain advantages.
- **Pacing:** Rooms cluster around the Main Hall, with the Treasure Room and Secret Passage offering distant rewards for deeper exploration.
- **Realism:** The layout reflects a functional yet ominous space, with rooms logically placed to serve the dungeon's sinister operations.
- **Vertical Space:** Although not explicitly vertical, the secret passage offers a layered experience by providing an alternate route.
