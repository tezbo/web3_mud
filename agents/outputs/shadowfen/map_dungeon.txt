### Room List with Exits

1. **Chamber of Echoes**
   - Exits: Down (D) to Mossy Antechamber
   - Notes: Central hub of the dungeon with natural acoustics enhancing eerie sounds. This room sets the theme and atmosphere of the Shadowfen. 

2. **Mossy Antechamber**
   - Exits: North (N) to Chamber of Echoes, East (E) to Hidden Alcove, Down (D) to Silt-Flooded Passage
   - Notes: A transitional zone connecting the Chamber of Echoes to deeper parts. The damp air and moss hint at the dungeon's age and neglect.

3. **Hidden Alcove**
   - Exits: West (W) to Mossy Antechamber
   - Notes: A concealed nook rewarding explorers with rare items or lore snippets. Accessible only by keen-eyed adventurers.

4. **Silt-Flooded Passage**
   - Exits: Up (U) to Mossy Antechamber, Down (D) to Slime-Covered Stairwell
   - Notes: A slippery, waterlogged corridor challenging players with movement penalties, enhancing the treacherous feel.

5. **Slime-Covered Stairwell**
   - Exits: Up (U) to Silt-Flooded Passage, Down (D) to Fungal Vestibule
   - Notes: A vertical transition space, the slick stairs test balance and introduce organic hazards, such as creeping vines and fungi.

6. **Fungal Vestibule**
   - Exits: Up (U) to Slime-Covered Stairwell, East (E) to Treasure Lair
   - Notes: A lush, bioluminescent area teeming with oversized mushrooms. Acts as the final resting point before the hidden Treasure Lair.

7. **Treasure Lair**
   - Exits: West (W) to Fungal Vestibule
   - Notes: A secret chamber containing treasures and potential boss encounters. The reward for those who traverse the entire dungeon.

### Design Notes on Flow and Purpose

- **Flow and Exploration**: The dungeon's design encourages players to delve deeper with a clear vertical progression, while also rewarding lateral exploration with the Hidden Alcove.
- **Atmosphere and Theming**: Each room contributes to the Shadowfen's distinct vibeâ€”moist, echoing, and overgrown with nature reclaiming the space.
- **Discovery and Reward**: The Hidden Alcove and Treasure Lair provide motivation for exploration beyond the main path, with both practical and narrative rewards.
- **Pacing**: The design moves players from open spaces to more confined, challenging environments, creating a sense of progression and depth.
- **Realism and Immersion**: The logical sequence of rooms supports a narrative of an abandoned, nature-overrun dungeon, promoting immersion in the Shadowfen theme.
