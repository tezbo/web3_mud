### Shadowfen Dungeon Map

```
         [Entrance]
            |
      [Guard Post]
       /       \
  [Central Chamber]---[Treasure Vault]
       \       /
       [Prison Cells]
           |
       [Secret Tunnel]

```

### Room List and Exits

1. **Entrance**
   - **Exits:** South to Guard Post
   - **Purpose:** Entry point to the dungeon, sets the tone with oppressive architecture and dim lighting.

2. **Guard Post**
   - **Exits:** North to Entrance, South to Central Chamber
   - **Purpose:** Acts as a checkpoint. Guards were stationed here, remnants of their duty still evident. It serves as a transition from the outside world to the dungeon's depths.

3. **Central Chamber**
   - **Exits:** North to Guard Post, East to Treasure Vault, South to Prison Cells
   - **Purpose:** The main hub of the dungeon, connecting to important locations. It provides a sense of scale and significance, with ancient carvings hinting at the dungeon's history.

4. **Treasure Vault**
   - **Exits:** West to Central Chamber
   - **Purpose:** A reward for explorers, guarded by traps and puzzles. Contains lore items and valuable treasures, offering incentive for those who delve deep.

5. **Prison Cells**
   - **Exits:** North to Central Chamber, South to Secret Tunnel
   - **Purpose:** Holds the echoes of captured souls. Players may find clues or keys here, with interactive elements like old chains and scattered skeletons.

6. **Secret Tunnel**
   - **Exits:** North to Prison Cells
   - **Purpose:** A hidden path for those observant or lucky enough to discover it. Offers an escape route or shortcut, possibly leading to other parts of Shadowfen in future expansions.

### Design Notes

- **Flow:** The Central Chamber serves as the primary hub, ensuring players naturally pass through it to reach significant areas like the Treasure Vault or the Prison Cells. This design encourages exploration while maintaining a clear route.
  
- **Discovery:** The Secret Tunnel rewards players who explore the Prison Cells thoroughly, offering a shortcut and a sense of mystery. The Treasure Vault is a clear goal, enticing players with the promise of reward.

- **Pacing:** The layout encourages players to progress from the mundane (Guard Post) to the mysterious (Secret Tunnel), providing a gradual build-up in atmosphere and narrative depth.

- **Realism:** The design reflects a defensive structure with logical placementsâ€”guards first, main activities central, and valuables hidden away. The Prison Cells are placed to suggest their secondary importance to the structure's original purpose.

- **Shadowfen Style:** The dungeon reflects the eerie and mysterious nature of Shadowfen, with rooms that tell a story of past use and abandonment, enhanced by atmospheric descriptions and subtle narrative clues.
