**Quest Title: "The Rot Beneath"**

**Stage Breakdown:**

1. **Stage 1: The Plague's Whisper**
   - The player arrives in Shadowfen, a swampy region plagued by a mysterious disease. Locals whisper of a sinister figure known as the High Priest of Rot who is rumored to be the source. The player meets Elda, a herbalist whose daughter is afflicted. Elda asks for help, offering a map to the High Priest's lair. 

2. **Stage 2: Into the Rot**
   - The player ventures into the murky depths of Shadowfen, facing natural hazards and corrupted creatures. Along the way, they encounter Derin, a defector from the Priest's cult, who offers crucial information: the source of the rot is a cursed artifact the Priest uses to amplify his power. Derin also reveals a secret passage into the lair.

3. **Stage 3: Confrontation at the Heart of Decay**
   - The player reaches the High Priest's sanctuary, a crumbling temple overrun with rot. Here, they must make a choice on how to confront the Priest and the artifact.

**Solutions:**

1. **Direct Confrontation**
   - The player storms the temple and battles the High Priest directly. Upon defeating him, they destroy the cursed artifact, purging the rot from the land.
   - *Consequences:* The rot recedes, and Shadowfen slowly heals. Elda's daughter recovers, and the player is celebrated as a hero. However, remnants of the cult scatter, potentially leading to future threats.

2. **Cunning Deceit**
   - The player uses Derin's information to infiltrate the temple via the secret passage. Once inside, they sabotage the artifact, weakening the High Priest before confronting him.
   - *Consequences:* The weakened Priest is defeated with less resistance, and the cult disbands. The player gains the gratitude of both Elda and Derin, who becomes a valuable ally. However, the sabotage leaves lingering magical instability, causing unpredictable phenomena in the region.

   - *Consequences:* The High Priest is overthrown by his own followers, who choose a new, more peaceful leader. Shadowfen begins to recover, but the new cult leadership seeks a tentative alliance with the player, complicating future dealings with other factions wary of the cult's past.
