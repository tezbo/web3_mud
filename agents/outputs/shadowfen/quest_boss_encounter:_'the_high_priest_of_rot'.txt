**Quest Title: The Rot Within**

**Stage Breakdown:**

1. **The Plague's Whisper:** 
   - The player arrives in Shadowfen, where the air is thick with decay and whispers of a deadly plague. Villagers speak of a High Priest of Rot who has taken refuge in the ancient crypts, spreading disease. The player is approached by Sister Elara, a healer desperate to save her people. She believes the High Priest holds the key to either unleashing or containing the plague.

2. **Into the Crypts:** 
   - The player must navigate the treacherous crypts, facing rotting abominations and traps set by the High Priestâ€™s minions. Along the way, they discover the priest's journal, revealing his tragic past and his belief that the plague is a test from the gods.

3. **Confrontation with the High Priest:** 
   - The player finally confronts the High Priest of Rot, a gaunt figure radiating a sickly aura. Here, they must make a choice that will determine the fate of Shadowfen.

**Solutions:**

1. **Purification by Steel:**
   - **Choice:** The player chooses to defeat the High Priest in combat, believing that only his death will end the plague.
   - **Consequence:** With the High Priest's defeat, the immediate threat of the plague diminishes, and the villagers rejoice. However, remnants of his followers scatter, and whispers of the plague's return linger, leaving the player with potential future encounters against fanatics trying to resurrect his teachings.

2. **The Path of Redemption:**
   - **Choice:** The player uses the information from the journal to convince the High Priest that his path is misguided, offering him a chance at redemption.
   - **Consequence:** The High Priest agrees to help reverse the plague, and the villagers are healed. The priest becomes an ally, aiding the player in future quests with his knowledge of diseases. However, some villagers remain distrustful, leading to tension and potential unrest in Shadowfen.

3. **Embrace the Rot:**
   - **Choice:** The player sees the potential in the High Priest's beliefs, deciding to join forces and harness the plague for their own ends.
   - **Consequence:** The player gains powerful abilities related to disease magic but becomes a pariah in the eyes of most of Shadowfen and surrounding regions. The player will face hostility from those who seek to stop the spread of the plague, and their reputation will shift towards infamy, affecting future interactions and quest options significantly.

This quest intertwines personal choice with the broader narrative of Shadowfen, offering players a memorable and impactful experience.
