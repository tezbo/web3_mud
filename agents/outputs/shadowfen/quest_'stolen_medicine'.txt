**Quest Title:** The Cure's Cost

**Stage Breakdown:**

1. **Discovery:**
   - The player arrives in the swampy region of Shadowfen and learns from a frantic herbalist, Elara, that a shipment of rare medicinal herbs needed to treat a rampant swamp fever has been stolen. Without it, many in the village will perish.

2. **Investigation:**
   - The player must gather information on the theft. Clues can be found by speaking with locals, examining the trade route, and questioning a suspicious merchant, Darin, who recently acquired a large sum of money.

3. **Resolution:**
   - The player uncovers that a group known as the Marsh Blades, desperate and impoverished, stole the herbs to treat their own sick community. The player must decide how to resolve the situation.

**Solutions:**

1. **Negotiate a Trade:**
   - The player can choose to negotiate with the Marsh Blades, arranging a trade of other supplies or finding alternative herbs to help both communities.
   - **Consequences:** Both communities receive some aid, fostering future cooperation. The player gains reputation as a mediator, leading to future quests where both communities seek the playerâ€™s help.

2. **Side with the Herbalist:**
   - The player retrieves the stolen herbs by force or stealth, returning them to Elara to save the Shadowfen villagers.
   - **Consequences:** Shadowfen is saved, but the Marsh Blades suffer, leading to increased hostilities. Later, the player might face ambushes or sabotage from the desperate group seeking revenge.

3. **Aid the Marsh Blades:**
   - Sympathizing with the Marsh Blades, the player can choose to let them keep the herbs and work with Elara to find an alternative cure for Shadowfen.
   - **Consequences:** The Marsh Blades are grateful and offer future assistance, possibly providing unique herbs or information. However, Shadowfen faces a slower recovery, causing tension and mistrust towards the player.

**Ties to World Lore:**
- The quest touches on the aftermath of The Sundering, which has left many communities like the Marsh Blades struggling.
- The use of herbal remedies reflects the ancient art of Threadsinging, hinting that Elara might possess deeper knowledge that could be explored in future quests.
