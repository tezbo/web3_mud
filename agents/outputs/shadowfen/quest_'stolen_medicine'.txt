**Quest Title: The Cure's Dilemma**

**Stage Breakdown:**

1. **Discovery of the Theft:**
   - While traveling through Shadowfen, the player encounters a distressed herbalist named Elara. Her medicine shipment, crucial for treating a deadly swamp fever affecting the local village of Mirepool, has been stolen.

2. **Investigation:**
   - Elara suspects the local bandit group, the Bog Shadows, who have been known to intercept supplies. The player must gather information on the whereabouts of the stolen medicine.

3. **Resolution:**
   - The player learns the medicine is hidden in the Bog Shadows' camp, but it's also revealed that the bandits stole it to treat their own sick members. The player must choose how to resolve the situation.

**Solutions and Consequences:**

1. **Retrieve and Return:**
   - The player decides to stealthily retrieve the medicine from the bandit camp and return it to Elara.
   - *Consequences:* Mirepool is saved, and the villagers are grateful, offering discounts and aid to the player in future endeavors. However, the Bog Shadows become hostile, increasing bandit encounters in Shadowfen.

2. **Negotiate a Truce:**
   - The player mediates a truce between Elara and the Bog Shadows, suggesting a share of the medicine in exchange for future cooperation.
   - *Consequences:* Both Mirepool and the bandits receive treatment. The player is seen as a peacemaker, resulting in reduced hostility from the Bog Shadows and the possibility of assistance from them in other quests. However, some villagers question the morality of aiding bandits.

3. **Side with the Bandits:**
   - The player sides with the Bog Shadows, allowing them to keep the medicine to save their own, arguing that the villagers have more resources.
   - *Consequences:* The bandits become allies, providing the player with unique items and information. Meanwhile, Mirepool suffers, and future interactions with its residents are strained, with some refusing to offer services to the player.

In all scenarios, the player's choices ripple through Shadowfen, affecting alliances and shaping the player's reputation in the region.
