**Quest Title: The Balm in Shadowfen**

**Stage Breakdown:**

1. **Discovery:**
   - Players arrive in Shadowfen, a swampy village plagued by a mysterious illness. They learn from Dr. Elara, the village healer, that a shipment of vital medicine has been stolen. The village is teetering on the brink of a health crisis.

2. **Investigation:**
   - Players must investigate the theft. Clues suggest that the medicine was taken by a group of swamp nomads, known for their distrust of outsiders. Alternatively, players could discover evidence pointing to a wealthy merchant in the village who may be hoarding the medicine for profit.

3. **Resolution:**
   - Players confront the chosen suspect: the nomads or the merchant. They must decide how to retrieve the medicine.

**Solutions:**

1. **Diplomatic Negotiation with the Nomads:**
   - Players approach the nomads peacefully, offering to mediate with the village in exchange for the return of the medicine. They learn that the nomads took the medicine out of fear, believing it to be a poison against their people.
   - **Consequences:** The village and the nomads establish a fragile peace, leading to future trading opportunities. However, a faction within the village distrusts the nomads, causing potential tensions.

2. **Expose the Merchant:**
   - Players gather evidence and confront the merchant, who is indeed hoarding the medicine to sell at an inflated price during the crisis. Players can choose to expose him to the village council.
   - **Consequences:** The merchant is disgraced and exiled, leading to a power vacuum in local trade. This opens opportunities for more honest traders, but the merchant's allies may seek revenge against the players.

3. **Retrieval by Force:**
   - Players decide to take the medicine by force, either from the nomads or the merchant, ensuring the village's immediate survival.
   - **Consequences:** The immediate threat is averted, but violence leads to distrust and fear. The nomads fortify their defenses, or the merchant's allies may rally against the players, causing ongoing strife.
