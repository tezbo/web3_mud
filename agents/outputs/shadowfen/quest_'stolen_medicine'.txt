**Quest Title: The Balm of Betrayal**

**Stage 1: The Discovery**
- **Hook:** In the murky swamps of Shadowfen, the local healer, Elara, is in distress. The rare medicinal herbs she painstakingly collected have been stolen, leaving the village vulnerable to the creeping swamp fever.
- **Objective:** Speak to Elara to gather information about the theft.

**Stage 2: The Investigation**
- **Objective:** Gather clues about the whereabouts of the stolen herbs. Locals mention seeing suspicious figures near the village outskirts and whispers of a black market dealer, Jarek, who may know more.

**Stage 3: The Resolution**
- **Objective:** Confront Jarek or investigate further based on gathered evidence.

**Solution 1: Confront and Intimidate**
- **Action:** Confront Jarek directly. Use intimidation or combat to retrieve the herbs.
- **Consequence:** The herbs are returned, but Jarek's associates will hold a grudge, leading to future ambushes or sabotage attempts in the region.

**Solution 2: Negotiate and Compromise**
- **Action:** Approach Jarek diplomatically. Offer a trade or favor in exchange for the herbs.
- **Consequence:** Jarek becomes an uneasy ally, providing information or assistance in future quests, but the village may be wary of your methods, suspecting collusion with the black market.

**Solution 3: Investigate and Expose**
- **Action:** Gather further evidence tying Jarek to the theft, presenting it to the village council to pressure him into returning the herbs.
