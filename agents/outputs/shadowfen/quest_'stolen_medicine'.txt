Title: "Cure and Consequence"

### Quest Breakdown:

**Stage 1: The Plight of Shadowfen**
- Hook: As the player enters the swampy village of Shadowfen, they are greeted by Elder Halwen, who explains that the village's medicine stock has been stolen. Without it, a deadly swamp fever will decimate the population.
- Objective: Investigate the theft by gathering information from villagers and searching the area.

**Stage 2: The Culprit Revealed**
- Through investigation, the player learns that a band of outcasts, led by a former villager named Rylor, is responsible. They stole the medicine to treat their own sick, having been exiled due to an old feud.

**Stage 3: A Decision to Make**
- The player locates the outcasts' camp, where Rylor explains their dire situation. The player must choose how to resolve the conflict.

### Solutions and Consequences:

**Solution 1: Side with Shadowfen**
- Choice: Retrieve the stolen medicine forcibly or negotiate its return completely to Shadowfen.
- Consequences: The village is saved from the fever, earning the player's respect and future discounts on goods. However, the outcasts suffer, and the player may face ambushes from them in future travels through the swamps.

**Solution 2: Aid the Outcasts**
- Choice: Allow the outcasts to keep the medicine, convincing or deceiving Shadowfen with a story of its destruction or loss.
- Consequences: The outcasts survive and later share their herbal knowledge with the player, granting unique alchemical recipes. However, Shadowfen suffers losses, and they distrust the player, leading to higher prices and less favorable interactions.

**Solution 3: Mediate a Truce**
- Choice: Broker a deal where Shadowfen shares part of the medicine in exchange for future cooperation with the outcasts.
- Consequences: Both groups benefit, leading to a future alliance that strengthens the region. The player is celebrated as a peacemaker and gains benefits from both sides, including occasional gifts or support in future quests.

### Ties to World Lore:
- The quest explores the lingering effects of The Sundering, highlighting how past feuds and current resource scarcity affect communities.
- The swamp fever is a result of the realm conflicts disturbing natural balances, a nod to the environmental impact of Threadsinging gone awry.

This quest allows players to navigate moral complexity, making a choice that resonates with the greater narrative of Shadowfen and beyond, ensuring their actions have tangible and lasting impacts in the world.
