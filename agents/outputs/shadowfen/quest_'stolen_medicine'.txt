3. **Confrontation and Resolution**: The player must decide how to handle the situation after determining the culprit.

Solutions and Consequences:

1. **Expose the Culprit**:
   - **Solution**: The player gathers evidence pointing to Eldrin, who wanted to discredit the healer and sell his own potions instead. The player confronts Eldrin with proof, convincing him to return the medicine.
   - **Consequences**: Eldrin is shunned by the community, impacting his business and social ties. The village's trust in outsiders wanes, leading to increased suspicion of strangers, including the player.

2. **Negotiate a Deal**:
   - **Solution**: The player discovers that Garvin took the medicine in a desperate attempt to save his child. The player negotiates with Garvin, offering to help find an alternative cure or convincing the healer to treat the child in exchange for the return of the stolen supplies.
   - **Consequences**: The village sees the player as a peacemaker, enhancing their reputation. However, the healer becomes wary of Garvin's actions, leading to strained relations between Garvin's family and the healer.

3. **Cover Up for a Greater Good**:
   - **Solution**: The player uncovers that Seraphine stole the supplies to help a hidden group of refugees suffering from the same illness. The player chooses to keep Seraphine's secret, helping her find more medicine for both the village and the refugees.
   - **Consequences**: The player's secret alliance with Seraphine boosts their standing with the refugees, potentially opening new quest lines. However, the healer becomes suspicious of the player, suspecting their involvement in the theft.

Each choice not only resolves the immediate crisis but also weaves into the broader tapestry of Shadowfen's societal dynamics and the player's evolving role in the world.
