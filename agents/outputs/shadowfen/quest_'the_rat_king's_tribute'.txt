1. **The Missing Tribute:**
   - The player arrives in Shadowfen, a murky swamp town, and learns that the annual tribute meant for the Rat King has gone missing. Without it, the town risks unleashing the Rat King's wrath, a fearsome entity revered and feared by the townsfolk.
   
2. **Uncovering the Culprit:**
   - The player investigates the town, gathering clues and speaking to the villagers. They discover three potential leads: a suspicious merchant, a rebellious youth, and a mysterious outsider recently seen lurking around the swamps.

3. **Resolution:**
   - The player must decide how to handle the situation based on their investigation, choosing between three paths to resolve the crisis.

**Solutions:**

1. **The Diplomatic Solution:**
   - **Action:** The player discovers that the mysterious outsider stole the tribute, intending to offer it to a rival swamp entity for protection. The player can negotiate with the outsider, convincing them to return the tribute by promising to mediate between the two factions.
   - **Consequences:** The town avoids conflict, and the player gains a reputation as a peacemaker. Future quests may involve mediating between different factions, and the player is given a token by the Rat King that allows safe passage through his domain.

2. **The Enforcement Solution:**
   - **Action:** The player concludes that the rebellious youth, feeling oppressed by the Rat King's demands, hid the tribute to incite change. The player can choose to expose the youth and return the tribute to the Rat King.
   - **Consequences:** The town breathes a sigh of relief, but the youth and sympathizers view the player as an enforcer of tyranny. Future interactions with rebellious factions will be strained, but the Rat King rewards the player with a unique item that grants temporary invisibility in the swamps.

3. **The Revolutionary Solution:**
   - **Action:** The player discovers the merchant orchestrated the theft, intending to weaken the Rat King's hold on the town by forcing them to confront their fears. The player can decide to support the merchant's plan, allowing the tribute to be used to rally the town against the Rat King.
   - **Consequences:** The town is inspired to stand up against the Rat King's rule, leading to a new era of independence but also potential chaos. The player is hailed as a hero by some and a disruptor by others. Future quests involve dealing with the power vacuum and the emergence of new leaders in Shadowfen.

**Tie to World Lore:**
The Rat King is said to be a result of The Sundering, an ancient event that transformed creatures and landscapes. His domain is a remnant of the old world, bound by the Threadsingers who once sang to keep such powers in check. The player's actions could influence how the Threadsingers view their role in maintaining balance in the realm.

**Stage 2: Into the Rat's Nest**
- The player ventures into the heart of Shadowfen to locate the Rat King's lair. Along the way, they encounter villagers with differing opinions on the Rat King; some view him as a necessary evil, while others see him as a tyrant.
- The player discovers the lair, where they are confronted by the Rat King, who offers them a deal: assist him in maintaining control over Shadowfen, and he will ensure their safe passage and reward.

**Stage 3: Decision and Consequences**
- The player must decide how to handle the situation with the Rat King.

**Solution 1: Ally with the Rat King**
- The player agrees to assist the Rat King in maintaining his rule. Together, they devise a plan to intimidate a rival faction threatening his control.
- Consequences: The Rat King's grip on Shadowfen strengthens. The player's reputation in the village suffers, but they gain access to exclusive resources and information from the Rat King. Villagers become wary of the player, leading to distrust in future interactions.

**Solution 2: Overthrow the Rat King**
- The player challenges the Rat King, rallying the villagers to rise against him and his band of loyalists. A tense showdown ensues in the swamp.
- Consequences: The Rat King's reign ends, freeing the villagers from his demands. The player is hailed as a hero in Shadowfen, but a power vacuum is created, leading to instability. Rival factions begin vying for control, setting the stage for future conflicts.

**Solution 3: Negotiate a Truce**
- The player mediates between the Rat King and the villagers, proposing a new agreement where the Rat King provides genuine protection in exchange for fair tribute.
- Consequences: A shaky peace is established. The Rat King's role shifts from tyrant to protector, but suspicion lingers. The player earns respect from both sides, with the potential to influence future developments in Shadowfen politics.
