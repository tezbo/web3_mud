**Quest Title: Shadows of the Past**

**Stage Breakdown:**

1. **Stage 1 - The Echoes of War:**
   - The player arrives in Shadowfen, a region haunted by the memories of the ancient conflict known as The Sundering. They meet Elandra, a Threadsinging historian, who is researching the history of the region. She has discovered that the spirits of fallen warriors are being used as pawns by an unknown force, causing chaos among the local settlements.

2. **Stage 2 - The Phantom's Call:**
   - Elandra requests the player's help in uncovering the identity of this force. The player must gather information from three key locations in Shadowfen: a ruined battlefield, an ancient burial ground, and a forsaken watchtower. Each location offers clues pointing to different enemy archetypes: The Phantom Lord, The Haunting General, and The Whispering Shadow.

3. **Stage 3 - Confronting the Past:**
   - With the gathered information, the player must decide which enemy archetype to confront. The choice will determine the nature of the final encounter and the method to resolve the quest.

**Solutions and Consequences:**

1. **Solution 1 - Confront The Phantom Lord:**
   - The player decides that The Phantom Lord, a spirit feeding on fear, is the one behind the disturbances. The player enters the realm of nightmares through a ritual, facing their own fears to weaken the Phantom Lord.
   - **Consequences:** Successfully defeating the Phantom Lord brings peace to Shadowfen, but the player earns a reputation for fearlessness, attracting both admirers and challengers from across the realm. Elandra gains new insights into the nature of fear and uses them to strengthen local defenses.

2. **Solution 2 - Challenge The Haunting General:**
   - The player believes The Haunting General, a warrior spirit seeking to recreate the battles of The Sundering, is responsible. The player must gather a band of allies from the local populace to challenge the General in a spectral battlefield.
   - **Consequences:** Victory over the Haunting General unites the people of Shadowfen, fostering a sense of community and resilience. However, some see the player as a warmonger, wary of their influence. Elandra chronicles the battles, inspiring future generations but also rekindling old rivalries.

3. **Solution 3 - Unmask The Whispering Shadow:**
   - The player suspects The Whispering Shadow, a manipulator sowing discord, is the true enemy. The player must gather allies to unravel the web of lies and deceit, exposing the Shadow's network of influence.
   - **Consequences:** Exposing The Whispering Shadow restores trust among the settlements, but the player becomes known as a master of intrigue, causing some to question their motives.
